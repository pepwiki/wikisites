## Overview

Endothelin Family Peptides are 21-amino acid peptides with potent vasoconstrictive properties.

## Key Members

- **ET-1**: Most potent endogenous vasoconstrictor
- **ET-2**: Similar potency to ET-1
- **ET-3**: Distinct receptor selectivity
- **Sarafotoxin**: Snake venom homolog

## Clinical Significance

Endothelin receptor antagonists (bosentan, ambrisentan) treat pulmonary arterial hypertension.
