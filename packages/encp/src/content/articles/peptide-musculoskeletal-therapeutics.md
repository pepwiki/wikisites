## Overview

Peptides targeting the musculoskeletal system include bone-building agents, joint protectants, and muscle anabolic peptides.

## Major Musculoskeletal Peptides

| Drug            | Indication           | Mechanism         |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Teriparatide    | Osteoporosis         | PTH analog        |
| Romosozumab     | Osteoporosis         | Sclerostin inhib  |
| Calcitonin      | Osteoporosis         | Bone resorption   |

## Clinical Applications

- Osteoporosis treatment and prevention
- Osteoarthritis management
- Fracture healing
- Muscle wasting
