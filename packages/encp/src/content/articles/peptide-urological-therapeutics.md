## Overview

Peptides have several urological applications, from treating overactive bladder to managing prostate conditions.

## Major Urological Peptides

| Drug            | Indication           | Mechanism         |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Desmopressin    | Diabetes insipidus   | V2 agonist        |
| Mirabegron      | Overactive bladder   | Beta-3 agonist    |
| Alprostadil     | Erectile dysfunction | PGE1 analog       |

## Clinical Applications

- Overactive bladder
- Urinary incontinence
- Erectile dysfunction
- Nocturnal enuresis
