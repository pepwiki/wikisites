## Central Mechanisms

### Appetite Suppression
- Hypothalamic GLP-1R activation reduces food intake by 20-30%
- Area postrema signaling
- Reduced food reward via dopaminergic pathways

## Peripheral Mechanisms

1. Gastric emptying delay: 20-40% reduction
2. Insulin secretion enhancement
3. Glucagon suppression
4. Energy expenditure increase
5. Lipid metabolism modulation
