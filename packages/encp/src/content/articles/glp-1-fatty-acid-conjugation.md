## Fatty Acid Conjugation Comparison

| Agent         | Fatty Acid     | Linker              | Half-Life  |
| ------------- | -------------- | ------------------- | ---------- |
| Liraglutide   | C-16 (palmitate) | Glu spacer         | 13 hours   |
| Semaglutide   | C-18 diacid    | miniPEG-OEG spacer | ~7 days    |
| Tirzepatide   | C-20 diacid    | Glu-Glu-Glu spacer  | ~5 days    |

## Structure-Activity Relationships

- **C-14**: Insufficient albumin binding
- **C-16**: Moderate binding (liraglutide)
- **C-18**: Strong binding (semaglutide)
- **C-20**: Very strong binding (tirzepatide)
