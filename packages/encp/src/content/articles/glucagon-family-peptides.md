## Overview

Glucagon Family Peptides regulate metabolism through incretin and counter-regulatory hormone signaling.

## Key Members

- **Glucagon**: Counter-regulatory hormone raising blood glucose
- **GLP-1**: Incretin hormone enhancing insulin secretion
- **GLP-2**: Intestinal growth and function
- **GIP**: Glucose-dependent insulinotropic peptide

## Clinical Significance

GLP-1 receptor agonists are major therapeutics for type 2 diabetes and obesity.
