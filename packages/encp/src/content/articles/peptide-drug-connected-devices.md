## Overview

Connected devices combine peptide drug delivery with digital technology for remote monitoring and improved adherence.

## Connected Device Types

### Smart Pens
- Bluetooth connectivity
- Dose tracking apps
- Reminder notifications

### Connected Autoinjectors
- Injection confirmation
- Usage data logging
- HCP notifications

### On-Body Sensors
- Continuous glucose monitoring
- Drug level monitoring

## Data Applications

- Adherence monitoring
- Dose optimization
- Real-world evidence
- Clinical trial support
