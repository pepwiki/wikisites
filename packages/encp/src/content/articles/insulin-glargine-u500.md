## Structure

Insulin glargine U500 uses the same glargine molecule but at five times the standard concentration. The high concentration alters pharmacokinetics significantly, producing both prandial and basal insulin effects.

## Clinical Applications

Indicated for diabetes requiring large insulin doses. The U500 concentration reduces injection volume by 60%, improving patient comfort and adherence.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Half-life**: ~30 hours
- **Onset**: ~6 hours
- **Peak**: 8-12 hours
- **Duration**: >30 hours
