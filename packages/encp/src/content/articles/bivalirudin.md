# Bivalirudin

## Overview

Direct thrombin inhibitor bivalent hirudin analog for PCI and heparin-induced thrombocytopenia.

## Mechanism of Action

Bivalent binding to thrombin active site and exosite-1 provides potent reversible inhibition.
