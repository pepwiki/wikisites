## Overview

Glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine copper(II) complex. Tripeptide (GHK) chelates Cu²⁺ with high affinity (Kd ~10⁻¹⁸ M). Stimulates collagen synthesis, glycosaminoglycan production, and superoxide dismutase activity. Promotes wound healing, reduces scarring. Used in skincare products (Copper Peptide GHK-Cu). Concentration: 0.01-1% in formulations.
