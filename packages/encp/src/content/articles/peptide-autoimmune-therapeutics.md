## Overview

Tolerogenic peptides represent a promising approach to treat autoimmune diseases by inducing antigen-specific immune tolerance without broad immunosuppression.

## Clinical Applications

| Disease           | Peptide Target       | Clinical Status   |
| ----------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Type 1 diabetes   | Insulin/GAD65 epitopes| Phase 2/3        |
| Multiple sclerosis| Myelin peptides      | Phase 2/3         |
| Rheumatoid arthritis| Collagen epitopes  | Phase 2           |
