## Overview

Reprocessing and reworking are controlled procedures for recovering peptide drug products that do not meet specifications.

## Definitions

### Reprocessing
- Incorporating failed batches into subsequent batches
- Following established procedures

### Reworking
- Processing failed batch through additional steps
- Requires regulatory approval

## Controls Required

- Deviation investigation
- Root cause analysis
- Risk assessment
- Quality approval
- Documentation
