## Overview

Peptide patents protect composition of matter, manufacturing processes, formulations, and methods of use for therapeutic peptides.

## Patent Categories

- **Composition of matter**: Novel peptide sequences
- **Process patents**: Synthesis and manufacturing methods
- **Formulation patents**: Delivery systems and excipients
- **Use patents**: Specific therapeutic applications

## Key Strategies

- Evergreening through analog development
- Formulation patent extensions
- Combination therapy patents
- Process improvement patents

## Patent Cliffs

Several major peptide patents are expiring, creating opportunities for generic manufacturers and biosimilar developers.
