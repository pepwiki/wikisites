# Phytosulfokine

## Overview

Phytosulfokine is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications. It is derived from Asparagus officinalis (Asparagus).

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Name | Phytosulfokine |
| Sequence | Y(SO3H)IYTQ |
| Length | 5 amino acids |
| Molecular Weight | ~850 Da |
| Source | Asparagus officinalis (Asparagus) |
| Category | Plant Signaling Peptides |

## Mechanism of Action

Receptor-mediated promotion of cell division and differentiation

## Bioactivity

Growth factor

## Therapeutic Potential

Plant tissue culture optimization, crop yield improvement
