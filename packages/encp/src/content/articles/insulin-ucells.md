## Overview

U-100 insulin contains 100 units per milliliter, equivalent to 3.47 mg/mL of insulin. It is the universal standard for insulin dosing.

## Dosing Equivalence

- **U-100**: 100 units/mL (standard)
- **U-200**: 200 units/mL (2x concentration)
- **U-300**: 300 units/mL (3x concentration)
- **U-500**: 500 units/mL (5x concentration)

## Safety Considerations

Look-alike/sound-alike labeling, dedicated pen devices, and patient education are critical safety measures to prevent concentration mix-ups.
