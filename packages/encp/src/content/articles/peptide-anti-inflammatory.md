## Overview

Anti-inflammatory peptides modulate inflammatory cascades by targeting cytokines, receptors, and immune cell recruitment.

## Mechanisms

- **Cytokine neutralization**: Blocking TNF-alpha, IL-1, IL-6
- **Receptor antagonism**: IL-1 receptor antagonist (anakinra)
- **Resolvin promotion**: Enhancing inflammation resolution
- **Neutrophil inhibition**: Reducing immune cell recruitment

## Examples

Anakinra (IL-1Ra) treats rheumatoid arthritis and autoinflammatory diseases. Thymosin alpha-1 has immunomodulatory properties.

## Clinical Significance

Peptide anti-inflammatories offer specificity advantages over broad-spectrum immunosuppressants.
