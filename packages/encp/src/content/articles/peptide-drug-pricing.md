## Overview

Peptide drug pricing reflects manufacturing complexity, clinical value, competitive dynamics, and payer willingness to pay.

## Pricing Factors

- **Manufacturing cost**: Synthesis and purification expenses
- **Clinical value**: Efficacy and safety advantages
- **Market competition**: Generic and biosimilar entry
- **Payer dynamics**: Insurance coverage and rebates

## Price Ranges

- Generic peptides: $50-500/month
- Branded peptides: $500-5,000/month
- Specialty peptides: $5,000-50,000/month
- Orphan peptides: $50,000+/month

## Trends

Value-based pricing, outcomes-based contracts, and competition from biosimilars are shaping peptide pricing strategies.
