## Overview

Peptide clinical development has progressed from simple hormone replacements to sophisticated targeted therapies across multiple disease areas.

## Milestones

- **1922**: First insulin clinical use
- **1970s**: GnRH agonists in cancer trials
- **1980s**: Cyclosporine transplant trials
- **1990s**: Enfuvirtide HIV trials
- **2000s**: Exenatide diabetes trials
- **2010s**: GLP-1 obesity trials
- **2020s**: Peptide drug conjugate trials

## Challenges

Short half-life, injection delivery, and manufacturing complexity have historically limited peptide clinical development.

## Advances

PEGylation, lipidation, and novel delivery technologies have overcome many historical limitations.
