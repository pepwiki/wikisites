## Chemical Identity

| Property          | Value                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Product Name      | Humalog Mix 75/25                         |
| Components        | 75% protamine lispro + 25% insulin lispro |
| Route             | Subcutaneous                             |
| Onset             | 15-30 minutes                            |

## Structure

Premixed 75/25 combines two physically distinct insulin species: protamine lispro crystalline precipitate for intermediate coverage and free insulin lispro for prandial control.

## Clinical Applications

Indicated for type 1 and type 2 diabetes requiring both basal and prandial insulin. Provides convenient twice-daily dosing with meals.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Onset**: 15-30 minutes
- **Peak 1** (lispro): 0.5-1.5 hours
- **Peak 2** (protamine): 4-8 hours
- **Duration**: 12-18 hours
