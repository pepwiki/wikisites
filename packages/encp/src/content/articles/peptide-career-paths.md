## Overview

The growing peptide industry offers diverse career opportunities across research, development, manufacturing, and commercial functions.

## Career Areas

- **Research**: Peptide design, medicinal chemistry, biology
- **CMC**: Process development, analytical chemistry, formulation
- **Clinical**: Clinical operations, regulatory affairs, pharmacovigilance
- **Commercial**: Marketing, market access, medical affairs

## Education Requirements

- BS/MS: Technical roles in manufacturing and QC
- PhD: Research leadership and design roles
- MD/PhD: Clinical development leadership
- MBA: Commercial and business development

## Skills in Demand

Peptide synthesis, analytical method development, GMP manufacturing, computational design, and regulatory expertise.
