## Overview

Analytical method development for peptides requires specialized approaches to address their unique physicochemical properties.

## Key Methods

- **RP-HPLC**: Primary purity and identity method
- **LC-MS**: Molecular weight confirmation
- **Amino acid analysis**: Quantitation and composition
- **Peptide mapping**: Sequence confirmation

## Validation Parameters

- Specificity and selectivity
- Linearity and range
- Accuracy and precision
- Limit of detection and quantitation

## Challenges

Aggregation, counterion effects, and multiple charge states complicate peptide analytical method development.
