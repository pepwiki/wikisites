## Clinical Evidence

### Semaglutide (Phase 2)
- NASH resolution without worsening fibrosis: 59% (2.4 mg) vs 17% (placebo)
- Reduced liver fat content by 70-80%

### Liraglutide (LEAN Trial)
- NASH resolution: 39% vs 9% (placebo)

## Mechanisms in NASH

1. Reduced hepatic de novo lipogenesis
2. Improved insulin sensitivity
3. Weight loss reducing visceral adiposity
4. Anti-inflammatory effects
