## Overview

Peptide formulation science has evolved to address the unique stability and delivery challenges of peptide therapeutics.

## Key Developments

- **1920s**: Simple aqueous insulin formulations
- **1950s**: Protamine zinc insulin for extended action
- **1970s**: Lyophilized peptide formulations
- **1980s**: Depot injectable formulations
- **1990s**: PEGylation for half-life extension
- **2000s**: Nanoparticle and liposomal formulations
- **2010s**: Oral permeation enhancer technology

## Impact

Formulation innovation has been as important as molecular design in enabling successful peptide therapeutics.
