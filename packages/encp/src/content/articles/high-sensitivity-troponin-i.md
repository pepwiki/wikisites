## Overview

Cardiac-specific troponin I (209 aa) measured by high-sensitivity assay. Released from damaged cardiomyocytes during myocardial injury. Normal: <0.04 ng/mL. ...n. More specific than CK-MB for acute coronary syndromes. Detectable within 3-4 hours of injury.
