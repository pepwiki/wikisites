## Overview

Cardiac-specific troponin I (209 aa) measured by high-sensitivity assay. Released from damaged cardiomyocytes. Normal: <0.04 ng/mL. Acute MI: >0.04 ng/mL with rise/fall pattern. More specific than CK-MB.
