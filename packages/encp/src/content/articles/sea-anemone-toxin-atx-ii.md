# Sea Anemone Toxin ATX-II

## Overview

Sodium channel modulator from Anemonia sulcata with cardiotoxic effects.

## Structure

| Name     | Sea Anemone Toxin ATX-II |

## Mechanism of Action

Slows sodium channel inactivation causing prolonged cardiac action potentials.
