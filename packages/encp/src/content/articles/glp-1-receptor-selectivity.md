## Overview

The GLP-1 receptor is a class B G-protein coupled receptor belonging to the secretin receptor family. Understanding its activation mechanism and selectivity is crucial for drug design.

## Key Structural Features for Binding

1. **Helical conformation**: Bioactive conformation is alpha-helical
2. **C-terminal docking**: C-terminus binds receptor ECD
3. **N-terminal activation**: N-terminus activates transmembrane domain
4. **Amphipathic helix**: Hydrophobic face critical for receptor engagement
