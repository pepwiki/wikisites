## Overview

CGRP Family Peptides are potent vasodilators and key mediators of migraine pathophysiology.

## Key Members

- **Alpha-CGRP**: Primary sensory neuropeptide form
- **Beta-CGRP**: Intestinal form
- **Adrenomedullin**: Vascular tone regulation
- **Amylin**: Metabolic co-regulator
- **Calcitonin**: Calcium-lowering hormone

## Clinical Significance

Anti-CGRP antibodies (erenumab, fremanezumab, galcanezumab) are FDA-approved migraine treatments.
