## Overview

Insulin products require careful temperature management throughout the supply chain. Most formulations must be stored at 2-8°C before opening.

## Storage Requirements

| Condition            | Unopened           | Opened              |
| -------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------- |
| Refrigerator (2-8°C) | Until expiry date  | 28 days (most)      |
| Room temp (<=25°C)   | 28 days (varies)   | 28 days             |

## Best Practices

- Never freeze insulin
- Use within 28 days of first puncture
- Discard if exposed to extreme temperatures
- Use insulated travel cases for transport
