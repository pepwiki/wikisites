## Overview

Continuous manufacturing offers potential benefits for peptide drug production.

## Batch vs Continuous

| Feature          | Batch Manufacturing     | Continuous Manufacturing |
| ---------------- | ----------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Production       | Discrete batches        | Continuous flow         |
| Flexibility      | High                    | Moderate                |
| Scale-up         | Complex                 | Easier                  |
| Quality          | Batch-dependent         | Consistent              |

## Continuous Processes

### Continuous Synthesis
- Flow chemistry reactors
- Automated monitoring

### Continuous Purification
- Continuous chromatography (SMB)
- Continuous crystallization

## Implementation Challenges

- Process understanding
- Equipment availability
- Regulatory acceptance
- Training requirements
