## Overview

The peptide market is driven by technological innovation, expanding indications, and shifting competitive dynamics.

## Major Trends

- **GLP-1 dominance**: Incretin-based therapies for obesity and diabetes
- **Oral peptides**: Delivery technology enabling non-injectable options
- **Peptide drug conjugates**: Targeted oncology applications
- **AI-driven design**: Accelerating discovery pipelines
- **Biosimilar entry**: Generic competition for established peptides

## Competitive Landscape

Novo Nordisk and Eli Lilly dominate metabolic peptides. Emerging biotechs are disrupting with novel mechanisms and delivery technologies.

## Investment Trends

Venture capital and pharma partnering in peptide companies has increased significantly, driven by clinical success stories.
