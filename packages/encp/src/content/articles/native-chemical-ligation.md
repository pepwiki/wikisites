## Overview

Chemical ligation method for protein synthesis. N-terminal cysteine of one peptide reacts with C-terminal thioester of another, forming a native peptide bond. Enables total chemical synthesis of proteins up to ~200 aa. Combined with expressed protein ligation for larger proteins.

This molecule plays important roles in biological systems and has potential therapeutic applications.
