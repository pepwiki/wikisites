## Clinical Applications

FDA-approved as Mounjaro for type 2 diabetes (2022) and Zepbound for obesity (2023). SURPASS trials showed HbA1c reductions of 2.0-2.6%. SURMOUNT-1 demonstrated 22.5% weight loss at highest dose.

## Dosing

- **Available doses**: 2.5, 5, 7.5, 10, 12.5, 15 mg
- **Dosing**: Once weekly subcutaneous
- **Starting dose**: 2.5 mg for 4 weeks
