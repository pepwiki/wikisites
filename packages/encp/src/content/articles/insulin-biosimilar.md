## Overview

Insulin biosimilars are follow-on biologic products demonstrating high similarity to an approved reference insulin product through extensive analytical, functional, and clinical comparability studies.

## Clinical Impact

Biosimilar competition is expected to reduce insulin costs by 30-50%, improving access for the estimated 500+ million people with diabetes globally.
