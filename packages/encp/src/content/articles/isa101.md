# ISA101

## Overview

ISA101 is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications.



## Mechanism of Action

Synthetic long peptides encompass full E6/E7 sequences; processed by APCs to generate both CD4+ and CD8+ T-cell responses against HPV16 oncoproteins
