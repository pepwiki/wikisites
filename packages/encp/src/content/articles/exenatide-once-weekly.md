## Structure

Bydureon encapsulates exenatide in PLGA microspheres that slowly degrade in subcutaneous tissue, releasing exenatide over one week.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Tmax**: 2-3 hours (initial), sustained over 7 days
- **Half-life**: ~5 days (effective)
- **Steady state**: 2-4 weeks
