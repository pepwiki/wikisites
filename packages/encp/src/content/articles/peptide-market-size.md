## Overview

The global peptide therapeutics market was valued at approximately $50 billion in 2023 and is projected to exceed $100 billion by 2030.

## Market Segmentation

- **By application**: Metabolic diseases, oncology, cardiovascular
- **By type**: Generic, innovative, branded
- **By route**: Injectable, oral, other
- **By region**: North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific

## Growth Drivers

- Rising diabetes and obesity prevalence
- GLP-1 receptor agonist expansion
- Peptide drug conjugate approvals
- Oral peptide delivery breakthroughs

## Key Statistics

- CAGR of approximately 9-11% through 2030
- GLP-1 agonists represent the largest segment
- Generic peptides growing faster than innovative
