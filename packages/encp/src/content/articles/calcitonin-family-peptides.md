## Overview

Calcitonin Family Peptides share structural features and regulate calcium, metabolism, and vascular tone.

## Key Members

- **Calcitonin**: Lowers blood calcium levels
- **Amylin**: Co-secreted with insulin, regulates glucose
- **CGRP**: Potent vasodilator and migraine mediator
- **Adrenomedullin**: Vascular tone regulation

## Clinical Significance

CGRP antibodies are breakthrough migraine therapies; amylin analogs treat diabetes.
