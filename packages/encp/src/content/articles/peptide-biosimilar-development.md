## Overview

Biosimilar development for peptides requires demonstrating high similarity to an approved reference product.

## Development Pathway

### Analytical Similarity
- Structural characterization
- Functional assays
- Quality attributes comparison

### Functional Similarity
- Bioassays
- Binding assays

### Clinical Studies
- Pharmacokinetic studies
- Comparative clinical trial
- Immunogenicity studies
