## Overview

Peptide therapeutics for neurological conditions face unique challenges including blood-brain barrier penetration and CNS stability.

## Major Neurological Peptides

| Drug            | Target          | Indication            |
| --------------- | --------------- | --------------------- |
| Ziconotide      | N-type Ca2+     | Severe chronic pain   |
| Lasmiditan      | 5-HT1F agonist  | Acute migraine        |
| Erenumab        | CGRP receptor   | Migraine prevention   |

## Blood-Brain Barrier Strategies

1. **Intrathecal delivery**: Direct CSF administration
2. **BBB-shuttle peptides**: Transferrin receptor targeting
3. **Nasal delivery**: Nose-to-brain pathway
4. **Focused ultrasound**: Transient BBB opening
