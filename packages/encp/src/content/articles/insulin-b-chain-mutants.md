## Key Mutants

- **PheB24->Val**: Loss of receptor binding
- **ProB28->Asp**: Lispro (rapid-acting)
- **ProB28->Lys**: Aspart (rapid-acting)
- **LysB29->Thr+acylation**: Degludec (ultra-long)

## Clinical Relevance

B-chain engineering underpins all modern insulin analogue development, from rapid-acting prandial insulins to ultra-long basal formulations.
