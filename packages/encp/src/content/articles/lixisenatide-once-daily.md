## Structure

Lixisenatide is a 33-amino acid exendin-4 analog with deletion of Pro38 and Pro39, and addition of six lysine residues at the C-terminus.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Half-life**: 2-4 hours
- **Onset**: 30 minutes
- **Peak**: 1-2 hours
- **Duration**: 6-8 hours
- **Dosing**: Once daily within 1 hour before the first meal
