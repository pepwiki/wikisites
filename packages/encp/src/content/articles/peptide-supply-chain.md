## Overview

Effective supply chain management for peptides requires specialized handling.

## Supply Chain Components

### Raw Materials
- Amino acids and derivatives
- Resins and linkers
- Solvents and reagents

### Intermediates
- Protected peptides
- Crude peptides
- Purified intermediates

## Cold Chain Requirements

| Product Type     | Storage Temp | Transport     |
| ---------------- | ------------ | ------------- |
| Drug substance   | 2-8 deg C    | Cold chain    |
| Drug product     | 2-8 deg C    | Cold chain    |
| Room temp stable | 15-25 deg C  | Controlled    |
