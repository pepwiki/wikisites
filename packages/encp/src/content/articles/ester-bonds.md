## Overview

Ester bonds in depsipeptides replace amide bonds, creating hybrid peptide-ester structures with distinct properties.

## Types

- **Depsipeptides**: Mixed amide-ester cyclic peptides
- **Lactones**: Intramolecular ester bonds in cyclodepsipeptides
- **Polyketide-peptide hybrids**: Natural product scaffolds

## Biological Significance

Depsipeptides include anticancer natural products (romidepsin, HDAC inhibitor) and ionophorous antibiotics (enniatin).
