## Overview

Precision medicine applies patient-specific data to select optimal peptide therapies, improving efficacy and reducing adverse effects.

## Key Approaches

- **Biomarker-guided selection**: Matching peptides to patient profiles
- **Companion diagnostics**: Peptide-based tests for treatment selection
- **Neoantigen vaccines**: Patient-specific cancer peptides
- **Pharmacogenomics**: Genetic factors in peptide response

## Applications

- GLP-1 receptor agonist selection based on patient characteristics
- Cancer neoantigen vaccines tailored to tumor mutations
- Allergen-specific peptide immunotherapy
- Rare disease enzyme replacement optimization

## Future Directions

Integration of genomics, proteomics, and AI will enable truly personalized peptide therapeutics.
