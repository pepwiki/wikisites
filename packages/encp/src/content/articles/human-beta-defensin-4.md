# Human Beta-Defensin 4

## Overview

Neutrophil-derived defensin with antimicrobial and wound healing functions at mucosal surfaces.

## Structure

| Property | Value |
| -------- | ----- |
| Name     | Human Beta-Defensin 4 |

## Mechanism of Action

Expressed in neutrophils and epithelial cells contributing to wound healing through angiogenesis.
