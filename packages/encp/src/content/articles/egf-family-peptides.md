## Overview

EGF Family Peptides bind ErbB receptors and regulate epithelial growth, differentiation, and survival.

## Key Members

- **EGF**: Prototype growth factor binding EGFR
- **TGF-alpha**: Autocrine growth factor
- **Amphiregulin**: EGFR ligand in immune regulation
- **Betacellulin**: Pancreatic beta cell growth
- **Neuregulins**: Neural and cardiac development

## Clinical Significance

EGFR-targeting therapies (cetuximab, erlotinib) are cornerstone cancer treatments.
