## Overview

92-aa calcium-binding protein (S100B) homodimer. Released by astrocytes upon central nervous system injury. Normal: <0.10 μg/L. Elevated in traumatic brain injury (proportional to injury severity), stroke, melanoma (tumor marker), neuroendocrine tumors. Prognostic marker in TBI.

## Clinical Significance

This molecule has important therapeutic or diagnostic applications in clinical medicine.
