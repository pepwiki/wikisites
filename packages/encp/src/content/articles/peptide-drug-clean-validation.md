## Overview

Cleaning validation ensures equipment is adequately cleaned to prevent cross-contamination.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Visual cleanliness
- Chemical limits (10 ppm, 1/1000th dose)
- Microbial limits
- Endotoxin limits

## Sampling Methods

- Swab sampling
- Rinse sampling
- Placebo sampling

## Validation Protocol

1. Cleaning procedure development
2. Worst-case product identification
3. Analytical method validation
4. Process validation runs
5. Statistical analysis
