## Overview

Effective intellectual property strategy is critical for protecting peptide innovations and maintaining competitive advantage.

## Protection Strategies

- **Composition patents**: Novel sequences and modifications
- **Method patents**: Synthesis and manufacturing processes
- **Formulation patents**: Delivery systems and excipients
- **Use patents**: Specific therapeutic applications

## Key Considerations

- Patent term extension opportunities
- Freedom to operate analyses
- Orange Book listing for ANDA protection
- International filing strategies

## Challenges

Patent eligibility for peptide sequences, prior art in public databases, and navigating generic entry.
