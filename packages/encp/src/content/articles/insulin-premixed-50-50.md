## Chemical Identity

| Property          | Value                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Product Name      | Humalog Mix 50/50                         |
| Components        | 50% protamine lispro + 50% insulin lispro |
| Route             | Subcutaneous                             |
| Onset             | 15 minutes                               |

## Structure

The 50/50 ratio provides a more prandial-dominant profile compared to 75/25, suitable for patients with significant postprandial hyperglycemia.

## Clinical Applications

Used in type 2 diabetes with prominent postprandial glucose excursions. Typically dosed twice daily with major meals.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Onset**: 15 minutes
- **Peak**: 0.5-1 hour (lispro), 4-8 hours (protamine)
- **Duration**: 12-16 hours
