## Overview

Secretin Family Peptides are structurally related hormones regulating gastrointestinal and neural functions.

## Key Members

- **Secretin**: Stimulates bicarbonate secretion
- **VIP**: Vasodilation and immune modulation
- **PACAP**: Neuroprotective and neuromodulatory
- **GIP**: Incretin hormone

## Clinical Significance

VIP and PACAP analogs are explored for neurodegenerative diseases.
