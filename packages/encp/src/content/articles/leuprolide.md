# Leuprolide

## Overview

GnRH agonist with D-Leu6 substitution for sustained LH/FSH suppression in prostate cancer.

## Structure

| Property | Value |

## Mechanism of Action

D-Leu6 substitution confers resistance to enzymatic degradation providing sustained receptor activation.
