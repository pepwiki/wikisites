## Overview

Peptide CDMOs provide synthesis, purification, and fill-finish services for pharmaceutical companies developing peptide therapeutics.

## Major CDMOs

- **Bachem**: Swiss-based peptide and oligonucleotide CDMO
- **PolyPeptide Group**: Global peptide manufacturing
- **Corden Pharma**: Peptide and lipid manufacturing
- **AmbioPharm**: Custom peptide synthesis
- **CPC Scientific**: GMP peptide production

## Services

- Process development and scale-up
- GMP manufacturing from gram to ton scale
- Analytical method development and validation
- Regulatory support and documentation

## Market Trends

Growing demand for peptide CDMO services driven by pipeline expansion and outsourcing trends.
