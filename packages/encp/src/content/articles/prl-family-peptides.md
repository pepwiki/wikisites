## Overview

Prolactin Family Peptides have diverse roles in lactation, reproduction, and immune function.

## Key Members

- **Prolactin**: Primary lactogenic hormone
- **Growth Hormone**: Structurally related somatogenic hormone
- **Placental Lactogens**: Pregnancy-specific hormones

## Clinical Significance

Hyperprolactinemia is a common endocrine disorder affecting reproduction.
