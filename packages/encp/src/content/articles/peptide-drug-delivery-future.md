## Overview

Future peptide delivery aims to eliminate injections through oral, transdermal, pulmonary, and implantable technologies.

## Emerging Platforms

- **Oral delivery**: Permeation enhancers and nanocarriers
- **Microneedle patches**: Painless transdermal delivery
- **Implantable devices**: Programmable long-acting release
- **Inhaled peptides**: Pulmonary delivery for systemic action

## Key Advances

- SNAC technology enabling oral semaglutide
- Dissolving microneedle arrays for vaccines
- Biodegradable implants with wireless control
- Exosome-mediated peptide delivery

## Impact

Patient-friendly delivery will expand peptide therapeutic use to chronic diseases requiring long-term treatment adherence.
