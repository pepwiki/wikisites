## Overview

Peptides play crucial roles in nephrology, from treating anemia of chronic kidney disease to managing mineral and bone disorders.

## Major Renal Peptides

| Drug            | Indication           | Mechanism         |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Epoetin alfa    | CKD anemia           | Erythropoiesis    |
| Darbepoetin alfa| CKD anemia           | Erythropoiesis    |
| Paricalcitol    | CKD-MBD              | VDR activation    |

## Clinical Applications

- Anemia of chronic kidney disease
- CKD-mineral bone disorder
- Hyperphosphatemia
- Renal osteodystrophy
