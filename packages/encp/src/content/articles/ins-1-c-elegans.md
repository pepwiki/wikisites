# INS-1 (C. elegans)

## Overview

INS-1 (C. elegans) is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications. It is derived from Caenorhabditis elegans.

## Mechanism of Action

Agonist of DAF-2 receptor, activates PI3K/AGE-1 signaling cascade, promotes phosphorylation of DAF-16/FOXO

## Bioactivity

Promotes dauer exit, reproductive growth, and lifespan regulation

## Therapeutic Potential

Aging and longevity research, insulin signaling pathway studies
