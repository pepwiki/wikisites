## Overview

Opioid Peptide Family members are endogenous ligands for opioid receptors, modulating pain and reward.

## Key Members

- **Beta-Endorphin**: Potent analgesic from POMC
- **Met-Enkephalin**: Ubiquitous pain modulator
- **Leu-Enkephalin**: Related enkephalin
- **Dynorphin A**: Kappa-opioid receptor agonist
- **Nociceptin/Orphanin FQ**: Distinct opioid-related peptide

## Clinical Significance

Understanding endogenous opioids informs addiction medicine and novel analgesic development.
