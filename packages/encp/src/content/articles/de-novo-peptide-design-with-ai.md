## Overview

De novo AI peptide design generates entirely new sequences rather than modifying natural peptides, expanding the accessible chemical space.

## Approaches

- **Generative adversarial networks**: Creating novel sequences
- **Variational autoencoders**: Learning peptide sequence space
- **Language models**: Treating sequences as text
- **Diffusion models**: Gradual structure refinement

## Applications

- Designing peptides for undruggable targets
- Creating peptides with non-natural functions
- Optimizing multiple properties simultaneously
- Generating peptides beyond natural diversity

## Challenges

Experimental validation, synthesis feasibility, and the gap between design and function remain challenges.
