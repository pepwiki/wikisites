## Formulation Science

PLGA microspheres encapsulate leuprolide in a biodegradable matrix. Upon subcutaneous injection, PLGA slowly hydrolyzes, releasing leuprolide over the designated period.

## Clinical Applications

Indicated for prostate cancer, endometriosis, uterine fibroids, and precocious puberty.
