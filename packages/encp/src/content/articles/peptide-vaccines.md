## Overview

Peptide vaccines use synthetic epitope peptides to elicit targeted immune responses against pathogens or tumors.

## Design Strategies

- **B-cell epitopes**: Surface-exposed linear or conformational epitopes
- **T-cell epitopes**: MHC class I and II presented peptides
- **Multi-epitope constructs**: Pan-variant coverage
- **Adjuvant conjugation**: Enhanced immunogenicity

## Applications

COVID-19 peptide vaccines, cancer neoantigen vaccines, and allergy peptide immunotherapy represent major application areas.

## Clinical Significance

Peptide vaccines offer safety, stability, and rapid design advantages. Neoantigen peptide vaccines show promise in personalized oncology.
