## Overview

Peptides have emerging roles in ophthalmology, from treating glaucoma to managing retinal diseases.

## Major Ophthalmic Peptides

| Drug            | Indication           | Mechanism         |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Latanoprost     | Glaucoma             | FP receptor agonist|
| Anti-VEGF       | AMD, DME             | VEGF inhibition   |
| Cyclosporine    | Dry eye              | Immunomodulatory  |

## Challenges

- Ocular bioavailability
- Topical delivery
- Retinal penetration
- Stability in eye drops
