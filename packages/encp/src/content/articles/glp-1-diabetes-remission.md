## Definition of Remission

- HbA1c <6.5% without glucose-lowering therapy for >=3 months
- Fasting glucose <100 mg/dL without medication

## Clinical Evidence

### Semaglutide (STEP Trials)
- Remission rate: 30-50% at 68 weeks
- Sustained remission: 20-30% at 2 years

### Tirzepatide (SURPASS)
- Remission rate: 40-55% at 40 weeks

## Mechanisms of Remission

1. Beta cell preservation
2. Weight loss reducing insulin resistance
3. Improved insulin sensitivity
4. Reduced glucotoxicity
