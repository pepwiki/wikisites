## Overview

AbbVie is a global biopharmaceutical company headquartered in North Chicago, Illinois, formed in 2013 as a spin-off from Abbott Laboratories. The company specializes in immunology, oncology, and neuroscience, with a focus on peptide-based therapeutics and biologics.

## Peptide Therapeutics

AbbVie's peptide portfolio includes leuprolide acetate (Lupron), a synthetic GnRH agonist used for prostate cancer, endometriosis, and precocious puberty. The company also develops peptide drug conjugates and novel peptide formulations for targeted delivery.

## Research Focus

AbbVie's research pipeline includes peptide-based approaches for autoimmune diseases, cancer immunotherapy, and neurological disorders. The company invests in computational peptide design and advanced delivery systems to improve therapeutic efficacy.
