## Overview

Cholecystokinin Family Peptides regulate digestion, satiety, and brain-gut signaling.

## Key Members

- **CCK-8**: Major brain form, satiety signal
- **CCK-33**: Intestinal hormone stimulating gallbladder contraction
- **CCK-58**: Circulating form in some species
- **Gastrin**: Structurally related stomach acid stimulant

## Clinical Significance

CCK-based satiety signals are targets for obesity therapeutics.
