## Management Strategies

### Dose Titration
- Start at lowest available dose
- Increase every 4 weeks (not 2 weeks)
- Slower titration reduces GI side effects

### Dietary Modification
- Smaller, more frequent meals
- Avoid high-fat, high-fiber foods
- Adequate hydration
- Upright positioning after meals

## When to Discontinue

- Persistent vomiting preventing adequate nutrition
- Severe weight loss (>5% body weight)
- Recurrent dehydration
