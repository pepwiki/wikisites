## Overview

Antiviral peptides interfere with viral life cycle steps including entry, fusion, and assembly.

## Classes

- **Entry inhibitors**: Block receptor binding or membrane fusion
- **Fusion inhibitors**: Prevent viral-cell membrane merger
- **Capsid assembly modulators**: Disrupt viral particle formation
- **Defensins**: Broad-spectrum innate antiviral peptides

## Examples

Enfuvirtide (T-20) is an HIV fusion inhibitor peptide. DJ peptide and EB peptide show broad-spectrum antiviral activity.

## Clinical Significance

Peptide antivirals offer resistance-resilient mechanisms, particularly relevant for emerging viral threats.
