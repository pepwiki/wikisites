## Overview

Immunomodulatory peptides fine-tune immune responses, offering therapeutic approaches for cancer, autoimmunity, and infection.

## Types

- **Thymic peptides**: Thymosin alpha-1, thymopentin
- **Cytokine-derived peptides**: IL-2, IL-7 mimetics
- **Tolerance-inducing peptides**: Autoantigen-derived peptides
- **Adjuvant peptides**: Enhancing vaccine responses

## Examples

Thymosin alpha-1 (Zadaxin) enhances T-cell function in hepatitis B/C. Glatiramer acetate is a peptide mixture treating multiple sclerosis.

## Clinical Significance

Peptide immunomodulators offer precision immune tuning with fewer off-target effects than small molecule immunosuppressants.
