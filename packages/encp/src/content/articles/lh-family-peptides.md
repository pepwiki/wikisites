## Overview

LH Family Peptides are critical regulators of gonadal function and steroidogenesis.

## Key Members

- **LH**: Triggers ovulation and stimulates testosterone production
- **hCG**: LH analog maintaining early pregnancy
- **FSH**: Complementary gonadotropin function

## Clinical Significance

LH surge is essential for ovulation; hCG is used in fertility protocols and pregnancy testing.
