## Clinical Evidence

### FLOW Trial (Semaglutide)
- Primary composite: 24% risk reduction (HR 0.76)
- Reduced progression to kidney failure

### LEADER (Liraglutide)
- New-onset nephropathy: 22% reduction

## Mechanisms of Renal Protection

1. Natriuresis: Direct tubular GLP-1R effects
2. Blood pressure reduction: 2-5 mmHg systolic
3. Anti-inflammatory effects
4. Anti-fibrotic effects
