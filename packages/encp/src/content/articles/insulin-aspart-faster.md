## Structure

Faster-acting insulin aspart (Fiasp) contains the same aspart molecule but includes niacinamide (vitamin B3) and L-arginine as excipients. Niacinamide promotes local vasodilation at the injection site, accelerating initial absorption.

## Clinical Applications

Approved for type 1 and type 2 diabetes. Provides faster onset than standard aspart, allowing dosing at meal start rather than 15-20 minutes before.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Onset**: ~2.5 minutes
- **Peak**: ~35 minutes
- **Duration**: 3-5 hours
