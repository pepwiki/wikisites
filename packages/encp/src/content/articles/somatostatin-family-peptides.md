## Overview

Somatostatin Family Peptides are inhibitory regulators of hormone secretion and neurotransmission.

## Key Members

- **Somatostatin-14**: Inhibits GH, insulin, and glucagon
- **Somatostatin-28**: Extended form with distinct receptor affinity
- **Cortistatin**: Neuropeptide with sleep-modulating properties

## Clinical Significance

Octreotide and lanreotide are somatostatin analogs used in acromegaly and neuroendocrine tumors.
