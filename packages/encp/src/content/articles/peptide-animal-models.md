## Overview

Animal models are essential for evaluating peptide drug candidates before human clinical trials.

## Common Animal Models

### Metabolic Diseases
- **Diabetes**: db/db mice, Zucker rats
- **Obesity**: Diet-induced obesity models

### Oncology
- **Xenograft**: Human tumor in immunodeficient mice
- **Syngeneic**: Tumor in immunocompetent mice

### Pain
- **Acute**: Hot plate, tail flick
- **Chronic**: Neuropathic pain models

## Translational Challenges

- Species differences in metabolism
- Receptor selectivity differences
- Pharmacokinetic variations
