## Current Recommendations

1. **Discontinue before conception**: At least 2 months before planned pregnancy
2. **Contraception**: Use effective contraception during therapy
3. **Breastfeeding**: Not recommended
4. **Alternative therapy**: Switch to insulin during pregnancy
5. **Postpartum**: May resume after breastfeeding
