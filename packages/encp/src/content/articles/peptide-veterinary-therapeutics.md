## Overview

Peptides have growing applications in veterinary medicine, from treating companion animal diseases to improving livestock productivity.

## Veterinary Applications

### Companion Animals
- **Diabetes**: Insulin therapy
- **Growth disorders**: GH therapy
- **Pain**: Opioid peptides

### Livestock
- **Growth promotion**: GH secretagogues
- **Reproductive**: GnRH analogs
- **Infections**: Antimicrobial peptides

### Aquaculture
- **Disease prevention**: Antimicrobial peptides
- **Growth enhancement**: GH and IGF-1
