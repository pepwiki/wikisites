## Overview

Phosphodiester bonds link nucleotides in nucleic acids and are used in peptide-nucleic acid conjugates.

## Types

- **PNA (Peptide Nucleic Acid)**: Pseudopeptide backbone with nucleobases
- **Peptide-oligonucleotide conjugates**: Drug delivery vehicles
- **Phosphopeptides**: Mimics of phosphorylated signaling intermediates

## Biological Significance

PNA is used in antisense technology, diagnostics, and gene editing. Peptide-DNA conjugates enable targeted nucleic acid delivery.
