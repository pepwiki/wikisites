## Overview

VIP Family Peptides are neuropeptides with potent vasodilatory, immunomodulatory, and neuroprotective effects.

## Key Members

- **VIP**: Vasodilator and immune modulator
- **PACAP**: Structurally related neuroprotective peptide
- **PHI/PHM**: Peptide with histidine and isoleucine

## Clinical Significance

VIP is being studied for pulmonary hypertension, Crohn disease, and sarcoidosis.
