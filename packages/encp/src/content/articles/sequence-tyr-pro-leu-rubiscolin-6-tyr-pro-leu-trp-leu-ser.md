## Overview

**Sequence:** Tyr-Pro-Leu (rubiscolin-6: Tyr-Pro-Leu-Trp-Leu-Ser) is a peptide compound with applications in research and therapeutics. **Sequence:** Tyr-Gly-Gly-Trp-Leu (exorphin C5); H-Tyr-Gly-Gly-Trp-OH (exorphin A5)

**Function:** Opioid agonist (δ-receptor); memory enhancement

**IC50/Activity:** Ki ~8.4 μM (δ-opioid)

First plant-derived δ-opioid peptide. The hexapeptide form shows memory-improving effects in mice via δ-receptor activation.
