## Overview

Soliqua/Suliqua represents a challenging co-formulation requiring compatibility between two chemically distinct peptides in a single delivery device.

## Formulation Challenges

- **pH compatibility**: Glargine requires pH 4.0; lixisenatide requires pH 5-7
- **Stability**: Both peptides must remain stable in combined formulation
- **Aggregation prevention**: Minimizing peptide aggregation over 28-day use
- **Dosing accuracy**: Maintaining correct ratio throughout use

## Dual-Chamber Design

- Chamber 1: Insulin glargine (100 U/mL)
- Chamber 2: Lixisenatide (33 mcg/mL)
- Mixing occurs during injection
- Fixed ratio maintained throughout use
