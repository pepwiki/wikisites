## Overview

PTH Family Peptides regulate calcium and phosphate homeostasis and bone metabolism.

## Key Members

- **PTH**: Primary regulator of calcium homeostasis
- **PTHrP**: Parathyroid hormone-related peptide in development
- **Tuberoinfundibular peptide**: Neuromodulatory function

## Clinical Significance

PTH analogs are used therapeutically for osteoporosis treatment.
