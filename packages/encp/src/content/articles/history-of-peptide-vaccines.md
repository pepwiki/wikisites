## Overview

Peptide vaccine development began with the identification of T-cell epitopes in the 1980s and has advanced to personalized neoantigen vaccines.

## Key Milestones

- **1980s**: MHC-peptide binding characterized
- **1990s**: First cancer peptide vaccine trials
- **1995**: Melanoma antigen peptides identified
- **2000s**: Multi-epitope vaccine designs
- **2010s**: Neoantigen discovery pipelines
- **2020s**: COVID-19 peptide vaccine candidates
- **2020s**: Personalized neoantigen vaccines in trials

## Impact

Peptide vaccines offer safety, specificity, and rapid design. Neoantigen approaches represent personalized immunotherapy for cancer patients.
