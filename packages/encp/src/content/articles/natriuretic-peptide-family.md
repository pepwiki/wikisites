## Overview

Natriuretic Peptides promote sodium and water excretion, reducing blood pressure and cardiac load.

## Key Members

- **ANP**: Atrial natriuretic peptide, released by atrial stretch
- **BNP**: Brain/B-type natriuretic peptide, heart failure marker
- **CNP**: C-type natriuretic peptide, vascular and bone effects

## Clinical Significance

BNP and NT-proBNP are gold standard biomarkers for heart failure diagnosis and management.
