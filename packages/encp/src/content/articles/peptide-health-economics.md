## Overview

Health economic evaluation of peptide therapeutics is critical for market access and reimbursement decisions.

## Economic Evaluation Methods

### Cost-Effectiveness Analysis (CEA)
- Incremental cost per QALY gained
- Willingness-to-pay thresholds

### Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA)
- Monetized health benefits
- Return on investment

## Key Economic Considerations

| Factor              | Impact                  |
| ------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Drug acquisition    | Direct cost             |
| Administration      | SC vs IV vs oral        |
| Monitoring          | Lab tests, imaging      |
| Adverse events      | Management costs        |
