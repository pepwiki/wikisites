## Overview

Rare diseases often involve deficiency of specific enzymes or hormones, making them ideal targets for peptide-based replacement therapies.

## Major Rare Disease Peptides

| Drug            | Indication           | Mechanism         |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Agalsidase beta | Fabry disease        | Enzyme replacement|
| Imiglucerase    | Gaucher disease      | Enzyme replacement|
| Laronidase      | MPS I                | Enzyme replacement|

## Challenges

- High cost ($100,000-$500,000/year)
- IV administration requirements
- Immunogenicity
- Access and equity
