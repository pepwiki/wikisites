## Overview

TSH Family Peptides regulate thyroid gland function and thyroid hormone synthesis.

## Key Members

- **TSH**: Stimulates thyroid hormone production
- **Alpha subunit**: Shared with LH, FSH, and hCG
- **TSH beta subunit**: Confers receptor specificity

## Clinical Significance

TSH is the primary screening test for thyroid disorders.
