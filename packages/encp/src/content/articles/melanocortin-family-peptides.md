## Overview

Melanocortin Family Peptides regulate pigmentation, energy balance, and inflammation through MC1-MC5 receptors.

## Key Members

- **Alpha-MSH**: Pigmentation and anti-inflammatory effects
- **Beta-MSH**: Energy homeostasis
- **ACTH**: Adrenal steroidogenesis
- **Gamma-MSH**: Cardiovascular regulation

## Clinical Significance

Setmelanotide is an MC4R agonist approved for genetic obesity. Afamelanotide treats erythropoietic protoporphyria.
