## Overview

## Chemical Identity

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Name | **INCI Name:** Pentapeptide-18 |
| Sequence | **Sequence:** Tyr-Ala-Gly-Phe-Leu

**Mechanism:** Targets enkephalin receptor on nerve endings; modulates acetylcholine release at the neuromuscular junction (different mechanism from Argireline)

**Application:** Expression line relaxers; often combined with Argireline for synergistic "botox-like" effect

Enkephalin-mimetic pentapeptide. Works at a different stage of vesicle release than SNARE-targeting peptides. Synergistic when combined with Argireline (different mechanism of action).
