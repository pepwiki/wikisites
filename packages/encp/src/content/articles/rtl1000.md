# RTL1000

## Overview

RTL1000 is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications.



## Mechanism of Action

Recombinant T-cell receptor ligand (RTL) consisting of MOG peptide tethered to MHC-II; selectively targets MOG-reactive T cells without broad immunosuppression; induces T-cell tolerance and Treg expansion
