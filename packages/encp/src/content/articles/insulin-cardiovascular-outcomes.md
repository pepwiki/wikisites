## Overview

Cardiovascular outcome trials have established the cardiovascular safety of modern insulin analogues, with some suggesting potential cardiovascular benefits.

## Key Trials

### ORIGIN Trial (Insulin Glargine)
- **Design**: 12,537 patients with CV risk factors
- **Result**: Neutral CV outcome (HR 1.02, 95% CI 0.94-1.11)

### DEVOTE Trial (Insulin Degludec)
- **Design**: 7,637 patients with type 2 diabetes and high CV risk
- **Result**: Non-inferior for MACE (HR 0.91, 95% CI 0.78-1.06)

## Clinical Implications

Modern insulin analogues are considered cardiovascularly safe, allowing clinicians to prescribe based on efficacy, hypoglycemia risk, and patient preference.
