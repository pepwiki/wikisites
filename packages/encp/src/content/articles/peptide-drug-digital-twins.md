## Overview

Digital twins are virtual replicas of physical systems that enable simulation, prediction, and optimization.

## Applications

### Manufacturing Process
- Batch simulation
- Real-time monitoring
- Predictive maintenance
- Process optimization

### Drug Product
- Stability prediction
- Dissolution modeling
- Bioavailability simulation

### Supply Chain
- Inventory optimization
- Demand forecasting
- Risk assessment

## Benefits

- Reduced experimentation
- Faster development
- Improved quality
- Cost savings
