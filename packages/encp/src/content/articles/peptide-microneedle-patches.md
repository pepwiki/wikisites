## Overview

Microneedle patches deliver peptides through the skin's outer layer using arrays of microscopic needles, enabling painless self-administration and improved patient compliance.

## Microneedle Types

| Type              | Material         | Mechanism              | Advantages         |
| ----------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------ |
| Solid             | Silicon, metal   | Skin puncture          | Simple design      |
| Coated            | Metal + peptide  | Coating dissolution    | Fast delivery      |
| Dissolving        | Polymer          | Needle dissolution     | No sharps waste    |
| Hollow            | Silicon, metal   | Liquid injection       | High dose capacity |

## Clinical Applications

- **Vaccines**: Painless vaccination programs
- **Diabetes**: Insulin delivery
- **Obesity**: GLP-1 agonist delivery
- **Hormone therapy**: Growth hormone, testosterone

## Advantages

- Painless or minimal pain
- Self-administration
- No cold chain (some formulations)
- Improved adherence
- Reduced needle-stick injuries
