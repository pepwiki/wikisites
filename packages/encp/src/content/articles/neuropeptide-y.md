# Neuropeptide Y

## Overview

Hypothalamic neuropeptide potently stimulating appetite and reducing energy expenditure.

## Structure

| Property | Value |
| -------- | ----- |
| Name     | Neuropeptide Y |

## Mechanism of Action

Most potent known appetite stimulant acting through Y1 and Y5 receptors in hypothalamus.
