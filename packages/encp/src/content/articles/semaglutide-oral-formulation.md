## Structure

Oral semaglutide uses SNAC to create a localized alkaline pH microenvironment in the stomach, promoting semaglutide absorption through the gastric mucosa via paracellular transport.

## Clinical Applications

FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes as Rybelsus (2019). The 14 mg dose reduces HbA1c by 1.0-1.4% and produces weight loss of 4-6 kg.
