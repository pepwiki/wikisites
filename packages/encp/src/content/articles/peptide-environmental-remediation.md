## Overview

Peptides with metal-binding or surfactant properties are being developed for environmental remediation, including heavy metal removal, oil spill cleanup, and water treatment.

## Applications

### Heavy Metal Remediation
- Phytochelatins and metallothioneins
- Metal-binding peptide sequences
- Biosorption to peptide-functionalized materials

### Biosurfactant Peptides
- Oil spill remediation
- Hydrocarbon degradation enhancement
- Soil decontamination

### Water Treatment
- Peptide-based filtration membranes
- Biofilm prevention
- Antimicrobial water purification

## Advantages

- Biodegradable
- Low toxicity
- Specific binding properties
- Renewable sourcing
