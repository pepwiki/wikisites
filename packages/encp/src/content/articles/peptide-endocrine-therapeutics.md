## Overview

Endocrine disorders are among the most common indications for peptide therapeutics.

## Major Endocrine Peptides

| Category        | Drug Examples          | Indication       |
| --------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------- |
| Diabetes        | Insulin, GLP-1 agonists| Type 1/2 diabetes|
| Growth          | GH, IGF-1              | Growth disorders |
| Thyroid         | Levothyroxine          | Hypothyroidism   |

## Clinical Considerations

- Hormone level monitoring
- Individualized dosing
- Combination therapies
