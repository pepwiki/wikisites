## Structure

Pasireotide is a cyclohexapeptide somatostatin analog with a broad receptor binding profile. It has 30-fold higher affinity for SSTR5 compared to octreotide.

## Clinical Applications

- **Cushing's disease**: FDA-approved (Signifor)
- **Acromegaly**: Off-label when octreotide/lanreotide fail
- **Neuroendocrine tumors**: Investigational

## Safety Considerations

- Hyperglycemia (common, 72%)
- Requires glucose monitoring
- May worsen diabetes control
