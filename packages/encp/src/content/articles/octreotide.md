# Octreotide

## Overview

Long-acting somatostatin analog with D-Phe and Thr-ol for acromegaly and carcinoid tumors.

## Mechanism of Action

D-Phe2 and Thr-ol modifications confer DPP-4 resistance and receptor selectivity.
