## Overview

Peptide supply chain management involves sourcing amino acids, resins, reagents, and managing cold chain logistics for finished products.

## Key Components

- **Raw materials**: Fmoc-amino acids, resins, solvents
- **Intermediates**: Protected peptide fragments
- **Finished products**: API and drug product
- **Cold chain**: Temperature-controlled distribution

## Challenges

Single-source dependencies, solvent availability, and cold chain requirements create supply chain vulnerabilities.

## Best Practices

Dual sourcing, inventory management, and quality agreements ensure reliable peptide supply.
