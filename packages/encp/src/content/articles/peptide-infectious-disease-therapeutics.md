## Overview

Antimicrobial peptides represent a new class of therapeutics for drug-resistant infections, offering novel mechanisms of action.

## Approved Infectious Disease Peptides

| Drug            | Class       | Target        | Indication      |
| --------------- | ----------- | ------------- | --------------- |
| Enfuvirtide     | Fusion inhibitor | HIV gp41 | HIV infection   |
| Daptomycin      | Lipopeptide | Membrane      | Gram-positive   |
| Polymyxin B     | Lipopeptide | Membrane      | Gram-negative   |

## Mechanisms

- **Fusion inhibitors**: Block viral entry
- **Membrane disruptors**: Kill pathogens directly
- **Immune modulators**: Enhance host defense
