## Overview

Tachykinin Family Peptides are neuropeptides involved in pain transmission, inflammation, and neurogenic responses.

## Key Members

- **Substance P**: Pain and inflammation mediator
- **Neurokinin A**: Bronchoconstriction and smooth muscle contraction
- **Neurokinin B**: Reproductive neuroendocrine function
- **Hemokinin-1**: Immune system modulation

## Clinical Significance

NK1 receptor antagonists (aprepitant) are antiemetics used in chemotherapy-induced nausea.
