## Overview

Peptide theranostics combine diagnostic imaging and therapeutic action in a single molecular agent, enabling personalized treatment.

## Approaches

- **Radiolabeled peptides**: Therapeutic isotopes on targeting peptides
- **Fluorescent peptides**: Imaging and photodynamic therapy
- **Peptide-drug conjugates**: Targeted delivery with imaging
- **Multifunctional platforms**: Combined modalities

## Applications

- Neuroendocrine tumor imaging and therapy
- Cancer-targeted radionuclide therapy
- Inflammation imaging and treatment
- Cardiovascular risk assessment and intervention

## Impact

Theranostics enable treatment monitoring, dose optimization, and patient selection for personalized medicine.
