## Overview

Peptide hormone replacement therapy treats endocrine deficiency disorders by supplementing deficient hormones with recombinant or synthetic peptide equivalents.

## Major Peptide Hormones

| Hormone         | Indication           | Route         | Frequency     |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| Insulin         | Diabetes             | SC/IV         | Daily         |
| Growth hormone  | GH deficiency        | SC            | Daily/Weekly  |
| Levothyroxine   | Hypothyroidism       | Oral          | Daily         |
| Testosterone    | Hypogonadism         | IM/SC/Topical | Weekly/Monthly|

## Clinical Considerations

- **Dosing**: Individualized based on levels and response
- **Monitoring**: Regular hormone level checks
- **Side effects**: Related to excess or deficiency
