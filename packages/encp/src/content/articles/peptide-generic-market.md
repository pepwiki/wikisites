## Overview

The peptide generic market is expanding as patents expire on blockbuster peptide drugs, creating opportunities for manufacturers.

## Key Segments

- **Insulin analogs**: Major generic opportunity
- **GnRH agonists**: Established generic market
- **GLP-1 agonists**: Emerging biosimilar competition
- **Calcitonin**: Mature generic market

## Regulatory Pathways

- ANDA pathway for peptide generics in the US
- Biosimilar framework for larger peptides
- Hybrid applications in Europe
- Reference product comparability studies

## Challenges

Demonstrating bioequivalence, managing immunogenicity, and competing on cost while maintaining quality.
