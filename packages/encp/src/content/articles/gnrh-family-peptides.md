## Overview

GnRH Family Peptides are decapeptides that regulate the hypothalamic-pituitary-gonadal axis.

## Key Members

- **GnRH I**: Primary regulator of LH and FSH release
- **GnRH II**: Neuromodulatory functions
- **GnRH III**: Found in fish species

## Clinical Significance

GnRH agonists and antagonists are used in fertility treatments, prostate cancer, and endometriosis.
