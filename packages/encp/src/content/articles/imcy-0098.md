# IMCY-0098

## Overview

IMCY-0098 is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications.



## Mechanism of Action

Cytolytic disease-specific peptides (CYSP) generate cytolytic CD4+ T cells that specifically kill insulin-presenting antigen-presenting cells; eliminates autoreactive immune responses without broad immunosuppression
