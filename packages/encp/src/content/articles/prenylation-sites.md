## Overview

Prenylation attaches farnesyl (C15) or geranylgeranyl (C20) isoprenoid groups to C-terminal cysteine residues.

## Types

- **Farnesylation**: CAAX motif recognition by farnesyltransferase
- **Geranylgeranylation**: Rab GGTase and GGTase-I modifications

## Biological Significance

Prenylation is essential for Ras, Rho, and Rab GTPase membrane localization and function. Farnesyltransferase inhibitors were developed as anticancer agents.
