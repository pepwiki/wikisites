## Overview

Neuropeptide Y Family Peptides are 36-amino acid peptides regulating appetite, anxiety, and energy balance.

## Key Members

- **NPY**: Potent appetite stimulant
- **PYY**: Satiety signal from gut
- **Pancreatic Polypeptide**: Regulates pancreatic secretion

## Clinical Significance

PYY-based therapeutics are being developed for obesity treatment.
