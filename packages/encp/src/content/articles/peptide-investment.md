## Overview

Investment in peptide therapeutics has surged, driven by clinical successes in metabolic diseases and expanding applications.

## Investment Trends

- **VC funding**: Record levels for peptide-focused biotechs
- **M&A activity**: Large pharma acquiring peptide companies
- **IPO market**: Peptide companies accessing public markets
- **Partnerships**: Pharma-biotech collaboration deals

## Notable Deals

- Novo Nordisk's investments in oral peptide technology
- Eli Lilly's tirzepatide commercial success
- Bicycle Therapeutics' platform partnerships
- Protagonist's collaboration with Janssen

## Investment Themes

Oral delivery, AI-designed peptides, and peptide drug conjugates are attracting the most investor interest.
