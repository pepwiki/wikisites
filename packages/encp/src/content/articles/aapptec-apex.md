## Overview

AAPPTec Apex is an advanced automated peptide synthesizer designed for solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS). It enables high-throughput peptide production with precise reagent delivery and temperature control for optimal coupling efficiency.

## Features

The Apex synthesizer supports both Fmoc and Boc chemistry strategies, with automated washing cycles, deprotection steps, and coupling reactions. It includes real-time monitoring of synthesis progress and automated cleavage/deprotection protocols.

## Applications

- Research-scale peptide synthesis
- Peptide library generation
- Custom peptide production
- Drug discovery and screening
