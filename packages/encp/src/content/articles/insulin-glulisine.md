## Structure

Insulin glulisine substitutes LysB3 for AsnB3 and GluB29 for LysB29. These charge-altering modifications destabilize hexamer formation, accelerating dissociation into bioactive monomers after subcutaneous injection.

## Clinical Applications

FDA-approved for type 1 and type 2 diabetes as a prandial insulin. Can be administered within 15 minutes before or 20 minutes after starting a meal.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Onset**: ~15 minutes
- **Peak**: 50-90 minutes
- **Duration**: 3-5 hours
