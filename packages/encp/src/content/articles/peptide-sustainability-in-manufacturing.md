## Overview

Sustainable peptide manufacturing aims to reduce solvent use, waste generation, and energy consumption while maintaining product quality.

## Green Approaches

- **Solvent reduction**: DMF alternatives and recycling
- **Water-based synthesis**: Aqueous coupling chemistry
- **Flow chemistry**: Reduced waste and improved efficiency
- **Enzymatic synthesis**: Biocatalytic peptide bond formation

## Challenges

Balancing sustainability with GMP requirements, regulatory acceptance, and cost-effectiveness remains challenging.

## Industry Initiatives

Major CDMOs are investing in green chemistry platforms and solvent recovery systems to reduce environmental impact.
