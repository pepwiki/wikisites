## Overview

Peptide quality control encompasses comprehensive analytical testing.

## Quality Attributes

### Identity
- Mass spectrometry (MS)
- Amino acid analysis
- Peptide mapping

### Purity
- HPLC (RP, IEX, SEC)
- Capillary electrophoresis

### Potency
- Bioassays (cell-based)
- Binding assays

### Stability
- Accelerated stability
- Long-term stability
- Stress testing

## Specifications

| Parameter       | Typical Specification |
| --------------- | --------------------- |
| Purity (HPLC)   | >=95%                 |
| Identity (MS)   | Confirmed             |
| Potency          | >=80% of reference    |
