## GLP-1 Receptor Signaling Pathways

### Primary Pathways
1. Gs -> cAMP -> PKA: Main pathway for insulin secretion
2. Beta-arrestin recruitment: Desensitization, internalization
3. ERK1/2 MAPK: Cell proliferation, survival
4. PI3K/Akt: Cell survival, metabolic effects

## Therapeutic Implications

### cAMP-Biased Agonists
- Enhanced insulin secretion
- Reduced receptor desensitization
- Potentially improved efficacy
