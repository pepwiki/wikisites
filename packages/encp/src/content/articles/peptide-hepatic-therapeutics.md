## Overview

Peptides have emerging roles in hepatology, from treating viral hepatitis to managing metabolic liver diseases.

## Clinical Applications

- Viral hepatitis treatment
- Non-alcoholic steatohepatitis
- Liver fibrosis
- Hepatocellular carcinoma
