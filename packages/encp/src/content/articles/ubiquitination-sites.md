## Overview

Ubiquitination attaches ubiquitin to lysine residues, targeting proteins for proteasomal degradation or modulating signaling.

## Chain Types

- **K48-linked**: Proteasomal degradation signal
- **K63-linked**: Signal transduction and DNA repair
- **Linear/M1**: NF-kB signaling
- **Monoubiquitination**: Endocytosis and histone regulation

## Biological Significance

The ubiquitin-proteasome system is a major drug target. Proteasome inhibitors (bortezomib) treat multiple myeloma.
