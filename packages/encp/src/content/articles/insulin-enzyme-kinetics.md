## Overview

The insulin receptor is a transmembrane receptor tyrosine kinase with high-affinity binding for insulin. Understanding binding kinetics is crucial for designing analogues with modified pharmacological profiles.

## Binding Parameters

| Parameter              | Human Insulin | Glargine | Aspart |
| ---------------------- | ------------- | -------- | ------ |
| Kd (affinity)          | 0.1-0.3 nM   | 0.2 nM   | 0.15 nM|

## Activation Mechanism

1. Insulin binds alpha-subunits of IR
2. Conformational change activates beta-subunit tyrosine kinase
3. Autophosphorylation of tyrosine residues
4. Recruitment of IRS-1/IRS-2 substrates
5. Activation of PI3K/Akt and MAPK pathways
