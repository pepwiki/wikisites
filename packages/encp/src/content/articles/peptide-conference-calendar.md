## Overview

The peptide community gathers at numerous conferences worldwide to share research, network, and discuss industry trends.

## Major Conferences

- **APS Symposium**: American Peptide Symposium (biennial)
- **EPS**: European Peptide Symposium (biennial)
- **IPSC**: International Peptide Symposium
- **TIDES**: Oligonucleotide and Peptide Conference
- **PepCon**: Peptide Conference series

## Topics

- Novel peptide therapeutics
- Synthesis and manufacturing advances
- Delivery technologies
- Regulatory updates

## Virtual Options

Many conferences now offer hybrid formats, increasing accessibility for global participants.
