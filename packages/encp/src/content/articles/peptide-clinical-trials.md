## Overview

Hundreds of peptide candidates are in clinical trials across metabolic, oncology, neurological, and rare disease indications.

## Trial Phases

- **Phase I**: Safety and dosing in healthy volunteers
- **Phase II**: Efficacy signals in target population
- **Phase III**: Confirmatory pivotal studies
- **Phase IV**: Post-marketing surveillance

## Therapeutic Areas

- Metabolic diseases: GLP-1 agonists, amylin analogs
- Oncology: Peptide vaccines, drug conjugates
- Rare diseases: Enzyme replacement therapies
- Neurology: Neuropeptide-based approaches

## Success Rates

Peptide drugs generally show higher success rates than small molecules due to their specificity and safety profiles.
