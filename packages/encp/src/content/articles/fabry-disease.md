## Overview

X-linked lysosomal storage disorder caused by α-galactosidase A (GLA) deficiency. Globotriaosylceramide (Gb3/GL-3) accumulation in endothelial cells, renal podocytes, cardiomyocytes, neurons. Manifestations: acroparesthesias (burning pain), angiokeratomas, hypohidrosis, corneal verticillata, left ventricular hypertrophy, renal failure, stroke. Treatments: enzyme replacement (agalsidase beta [Fabrazyme], agalsidase alfa [Replagal]), chaperone therapy (migalastat [Galafold] for amenable mutations).
