## Overview

Peptide-aptamer hybrids combine the diverse functional properties of peptides with the high-affinity target recognition of nucleic acid aptamers.

## Design Strategies

### Structural Integration
- Peptide-aptamer fusion constructs
- Chemical conjugation
- Modular assembly

### Functional Advantages
- Enhanced cellular uptake (peptide)
- High target specificity (aptamer)
- Improved pharmacokinetics
- Multi-valent binding

## Applications

- **Diagnostics**: Dual-mode detection
- **Therapeutics**: Targeted drug delivery
- **Imaging**: Molecular imaging probes
- **Biosensors**: Signal amplification
