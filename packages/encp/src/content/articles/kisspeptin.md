# Kisspeptin

## Overview

Hypothalamic neuropeptide essential for puberty onset and reproductive function.

## Structure

| Property | Value |
| -------- | ----- |

## Mechanism of Action

Activates GnRH neurons through KISS1R triggering pubertal development and fertility.
