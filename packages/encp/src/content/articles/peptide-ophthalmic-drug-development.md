## Overview

Developing peptide therapeutics for ophthalmic applications requires overcoming unique challenges related to ocular anatomy.

## Formulation Challenges

- Corneal epithelium barrier
- Tear film dilution
- Nasolacrimal drainage
- Enzymatic degradation

## Delivery Strategies

- **Topical**: Eye drops, gels, inserts
- **Intravitreal**: Direct retinal delivery
- **Periocular**: Subconjunctival injection
- **Systemic**: For posterior segment disease
