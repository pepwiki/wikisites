## Overview

Vasopressin Family Peptides regulate water balance, blood pressure, and social behavior.

## Key Members

- **AVP (Vasopressin)**: Antidiuretic hormone regulating water reabsorption
- **Oxytocin**: Structurally related nonapeptide
- **Vasotocin**: Non-mammalian homolog

## Clinical Significance

Desmopressin (synthetic AVP analog) treats diabetes insipidus and nocturnal enuresis.
