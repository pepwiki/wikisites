## Overview

Device development for peptide drugs requires integration of formulation science with mechanical engineering.

## Device Types

### Pre-filled Syringes
- Single-use devices
- Safety syringes with needles

### Autoinjectors
- Spring-powered devices
- Single-use or multi-dose
- Hidden needle designs

### Pen Injectors
- Multi-dose devices
- Dose selection dial
- Cartridge-based systems

### Wearable Devices
- On-body injectors
- Patch pumps
- Connected devices
