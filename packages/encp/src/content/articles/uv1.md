# UV1

## Overview

UV1 is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications.



## Mechanism of Action

Targets telomerase overexpressed in ~85% of cancers; generates CD4+ and CD8+ T-cell responses against hTERT-expressing tumor cells
