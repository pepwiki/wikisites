## Formulation

Triptorelin is available in microsphere depot formulations providing 1-month, 3-month, and 6-month sustained release. PLGA microspheres encapsulate the peptide for controlled subcutaneous release.

## Clinical Applications

- Advanced prostate cancer
- Central precocious puberty (children >=2 years)
- Endometriosis (off-label)
- Assisted reproduction (triggering oocyte maturation)
