## Overview

Fibroblast Growth Factors are signaling peptides regulating development, metabolism, and tissue repair.

## Key Members

- **FGF1/FGF2**: Angiogenesis and wound healing
- **FGF7 (KGF)**: Epithelial proliferation
- **FGF19/21/23**: Endocrine metabolic regulators
- **FGF8/10**: Developmental patterning

## Clinical Significance

FGF21 analogs are in trials for NASH and metabolic disease; FGF23 is key in phosphate homeostasis disorders.
