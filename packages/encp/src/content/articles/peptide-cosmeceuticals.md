## Overview

Peptide cosmeceuticals are bioactive peptides incorporated into skincare products for their purported anti-aging, skin firming, and wound healing properties. They represent a rapidly growing market segment.

## Common Peptide Cosmeceuticals

| Peptide         | INCI Name          | Claimed Benefit        |
| --------------- | ------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Matrixyl        | Palmitoyl Pentapeptide-4 | Anti-wrinkle       |
| SNAP-8          | Acetyl Octapeptide-3 | Muscle relaxing      |
| Eyeseryl        | Acetyl Tetrapeptide-5 | Anti-edema           |
| Syn-Ake         | Dipeptide Diaminobutyroyl Benzylamide | Muscle relaxing |
| Copper peptide  | Copper Tripeptide-1 | Wound healing         |

## Mechanism of Action

- **Signal peptides**: Stimulate collagen synthesis
- **Neurotransmitter-inhibiting peptides**: Reduce muscle contraction
- **Carrier peptides**: Deliver trace elements
- **Enzyme-inhibitor peptides**: Prevent wrinkle formation
