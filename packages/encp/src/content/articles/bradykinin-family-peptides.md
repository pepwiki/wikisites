## Overview

Bradykinin Family Peptides are vasoactive kinins involved in inflammation, pain, and vascular permeability.

## Key Members

- **Bradykinin**: Nonapeptide vasodilator and pain mediator
- **Kallidin**: Decapeptide with similar actions
- **Des-Arg-bradykinin**: Metabolite with distinct receptor selectivity

## Clinical Significance

ACE inhibitors increase bradykinin levels, contributing to their antihypertensive effects and dry cough side effect.
