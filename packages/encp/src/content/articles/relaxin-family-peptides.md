## Overview

Relaxin Family Peptides have diverse roles in reproduction, cardiovascular function, and tissue remodeling.

## Key Members

- **Relaxin-1/2**: Pregnancy hormone relaxing pelvic ligaments
- **Relaxin-3**: Brain-specific neuropeptide
- **INSL3**: Leydig cell hormone
- **INSL5**: Gut-brain peptide
- **INSL6**: Testis-specific function

## Clinical Significance

Serelaxin (recombinant relaxin-2) was investigated for acute heart failure treatment.
