## Overview

Comparability studies demonstrate that changes in manufacturing do not adversely affect peptide drug product quality.

## When Required

- Manufacturing site changes
- Equipment changes
- Process modifications
- Scale changes

## Framework

### Analytical Comparability
- Structural characterization
- Purity and impurity profiles
- Biological activity

### Clinical Comparability
- PK/PD studies
- Clinical efficacy comparison
- Immunogenicity assessment
