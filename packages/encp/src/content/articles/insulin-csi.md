## Overview

Continuous subcutaneous insulin infusion delivers rapid-acting insulin through a programmable external pump, providing both continuous basal rates and on-demand bolus doses.

## Pump Components

1. **Reservoir**: Holds 200-300 units of rapid-acting insulin
2. **Pump mechanism**: Stepper motor or piezoelectric actuator
3. **Infusion set**: Subcutaneous catheter
4. **Software**: Rate programming, bolus calculators, alerts

## Advantages

- Precise dosing (0.01-0.05 unit increments)
- Flexible meal timing
- Reduced glycemic variability
- Programmable insulin-to-carb ratios
