## Overview

Container closure system selection is critical for maintaining peptide drug product stability.

## Primary Container Types

### Glass Vials
- Type I borosilicate glass
- Best chemical resistance

### Pre-filled Syringes
- Glass barrel
- Cyclic olefin polymer barrel
- Silicone oil lubrication

### Cartridges
- Glass cartridges for pen injector devices

## Qualification Requirements

- Extractables and leachables studies
- Container closure integrity
- Compatibility testing
- Stability studies
