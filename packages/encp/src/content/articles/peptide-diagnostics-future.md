## Overview

Next-generation peptide diagnostics leverage nanotechnology, AI, and miniaturization for faster, more sensitive disease detection.

## Emerging Technologies

- **Peptide microarrays**: High-throughput multiplexed testing
- **Nanopore sequencing**: Single-molecule peptide analysis
- **CRISPR-peptide sensors**: Ultra-sensitive nucleic acid detection
- **Wearable biosensors**: Continuous peptide monitoring

## Applications

- Early cancer detection via circulating peptide panels
- Infectious disease rapid testing
- Neurodegenerative disease blood tests
- Point-of-care cardiac marker testing

## Impact

Decentralized testing and continuous monitoring will shift diagnostics from reactive to predictive medicine.
