## Overview

The peptide industry includes large pharmaceutical companies, specialized biotech firms, and contract development organizations.

## Major Pharma

- **Novo Nordisk**: GLP-1 agonists, insulin analogs
- **Eli Lilly**: Tirzepatide, incretin therapies
- **AstraZeneca**: Peptide drug conjugates
- **Sanofi**: Peptide vaccines and conjugates

## Biotech Leaders

- **Bicycle Therapeutics**: Bicycle peptide conjugates
- **Protagonist Therapeutics**: Oral peptide drugs
- **PepGen**: Peptide-conjugated oligonucleotides
- **Radius Health**: PTH analogs

## CDMOs

- Bachem, PolyPeptide Group, Corden Pharma
- Specialized GMP peptide manufacturing
