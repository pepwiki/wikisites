## Overview

Peptide-based crop protection offers environmentally friendly alternatives to chemical pesticides, utilizing natural antimicrobial and insecticidal peptides from various organisms.

## Applications

### Antifungal Peptides
- Control of plant fungal pathogens
- Alternatives to chemical fungicides
- Reduced resistance development

### Insecticidal Peptides
- Targeted pest control
- Bt toxins and related peptides
- Spider venom peptides

### Plant Defense Elicitors
- Activate plant immune responses
- Systemic acquired resistance
- Induced systemic resistance

## Advantages

- High specificity
- Biodegradability
- Low environmental persistence
- Reduced resistance development
- Compatible with organic farming
