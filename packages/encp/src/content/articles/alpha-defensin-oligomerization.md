# Alpha-Defensin Oligomerization

## Overview

Oligomerization forming pore-like structures in bacterial membranes.

## Structure

| Name     | Alpha-Defensin Oligomerization |

## Mechanism of Action

Concentration-dependent oligomerization creates transmembrane pores causing cell lysis.
