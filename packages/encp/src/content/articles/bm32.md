# BM32

## Overview

BM32 is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications.



## Mechanism of Action

Allergen peptides displayed on HBV-derived VLPs; VLPs are highly immunogenic and activate B cells via repetitive epitope display; induces IgG4-blocking antibodies without IgE cross-linking; promotes Treg responses
