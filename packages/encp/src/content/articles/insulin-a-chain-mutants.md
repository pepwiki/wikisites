## Key Mutants

- **GlyA1->Ser**: Reduced receptor affinity by 200-fold
- **IleA2->Val**: Modest reduction in binding
- **ValA3->Asp**: Abolished receptor binding
- **GluA4->Gln**: Altered receptor selectivity
- **ThrA8->Asp**: Reduced biological activity

## Research Applications

These mutants serve as tools for understanding insulin-receptor interactions and for engineering novel insulin analogues with modified receptor selectivity profiles.
