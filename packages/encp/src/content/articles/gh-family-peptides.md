## Overview

Growth Hormone Family Peptides regulate linear growth, metabolism, and lactation.

## Key Members

- **Growth Hormone (GH)**: Stimulates linear growth and metabolism
- **Prolactin**: Regulates lactation and reproduction
- **Placental Lactogen**: Supports pregnancy metabolism

## Clinical Significance

GH deficiency and excess conditions including acromegaly and dwarfism are well-characterized.
