**Mechanism:** SNARE complex modulator; inhibits vesicle docking by competing with SNAP-25 for SNARE complex assembly

**Application:** "Botox-like" wrinkle relaxers; eye contour, forehead serums; non-injection alternative to botulinum toxin

Hexapeptide derived from the N-terminal of SNAP-25. Reduces expression line depth by ~30% at 10% concentration over 30 days. Does not paralyze muscle; modulates neurotransmitter release.
