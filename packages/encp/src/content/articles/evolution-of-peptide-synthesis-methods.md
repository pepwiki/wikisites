## Overview

Peptide synthesis has undergone dramatic evolution from early solution-phase chemistry to modern automated solid-phase and flow synthesis approaches.

## Evolution Timeline

- **1900s**: Fischer's early peptide bond formation
- **1930s**: Bergmann carbobenzoxy protecting group
- **1950s**: Solution-phase synthesis of oxytocin
- **1963**: Merrifield's solid-phase revolution
- **1970s**: Fmoc/tBu strategy development
- **1990s**: Microwave-assisted synthesis
- **2010s**: Continuous flow SPPS

## Impact

Each advance increased efficiency, purity, and accessibility, enabling the modern peptide drug industry.
