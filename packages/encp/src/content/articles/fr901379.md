# FR901379

## Overview

FR901379 is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications. It is derived from Coleophoma empetri.

## Mechanism of Action

Inhibits fungal cell wall glucan synthesis; potent against resistant strains

## Bioactivity

Antifungal (broad spectrum)

## Therapeutic Potential

Precursor for micafungin (approved antifungal)
