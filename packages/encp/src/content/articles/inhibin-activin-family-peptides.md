## Overview

Inhibin and Activin Family Peptides are TGF-beta superfamily members regulating FSH secretion and cell differentiation.

## Key Members

- **Inhibin A/B**: Suppress FSH secretion
- **Activin A/B**: Stimulate FSH secretion
- **GDF9/BMP15**: Oocyte-specific growth factors
- **Follistatin**: Activin-binding protein

## Clinical Significance

Activin A is a biomarker for several cancers; follistatin-based therapeutics are in clinical trials for muscle wasting.
