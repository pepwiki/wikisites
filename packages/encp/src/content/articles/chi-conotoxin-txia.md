# Chi-Conotoxin TxIA

## Overview

Acetylcholine release inhibitor from Conus textile blocking presynaptic channels.

## Structure

| -------- | ----- |

## Mechanism of Action

Blocks P/Q-type calcium channels reducing acetylcholine release at neuromuscular junction.
