## Clinical Applications

FDA-approved for postmenopausal osteoporosis. The nasal spray provides an alternative to injectable calcitonin.

## Pharmacokinetics

- **Bioavailability**: 3-5% (nasal)
- **Tmax**: 15-30 minutes
