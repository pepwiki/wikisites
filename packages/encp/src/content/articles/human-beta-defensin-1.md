# Human Beta-Defensin 1

## Overview

Constitutively expressed epithelial defensin providing innate defense at mucosal surfaces.

## Structure

| Property | Value |
| -------- | ----- |
| Name     | Human Beta-Defensin 1 |

## Mechanism of Action

Beta-sheet structure stabilized by three disulfide bonds disrupts bacterial membranes through electrostatic interaction.
