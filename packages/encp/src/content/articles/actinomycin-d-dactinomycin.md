## Structure

Dactinomycin consists of a phenoxazone chromophore (actinocin) linked to two cyclic depsipeptide lactone rings containing unusual amino acids.

## Mechanism

Intercalates into DNA at GC-rich regions, inhibiting RNA polymerase and DNA-dependent RNA synthesis. The peptide rings bind in the minor groove.

## Clinical Applications

- Wilms tumor (pediatric)
- Ewing sarcoma
- Rhabdomyosarcoma
- Gestational trophoblastic disease
- Pediatric germ cell tumors
