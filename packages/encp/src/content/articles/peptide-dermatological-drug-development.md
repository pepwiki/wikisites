## Overview

Topical peptide therapeutics face unique challenges related to skin barrier function and penetration enhancement.

## Skin Penetration Barriers

- Stratum corneum barrier
- Molecular weight limitations
- Lipophilicity requirements
- Enzymatic degradation

## Enhancement Strategies

- **Chemical enhancement**: Penetration enhancers
- **Physical enhancement**: Iontophoresis, microneedles
- **Nanotechnology**: Liposomes, nanoparticles
- **Peptide modification**: Lipidation, cyclization
