# Hyp3-bradykinin

## Overview

Hyp3-bradykinin is a bioactive peptide with well-characterized properties and therapeutic applications. It is derived from Rana temporaria.

## Mechanism of Action

Hydroxyproline-modified bradykinin analog, altered conformation and receptor interaction

## Bioactivity

Vasodilation, modified biological activity due to proline hydroxylation

## Therapeutic Potential

Structure-activity studies, kinin analog development
