## Overview

Hypoglycemia unawareness affects 20-30% of type 1 diabetes patients and develops when recurrent hypoglycemic episodes blunt the autonomic counter-regulatory response.

## Pathophysiology

1. **Recurrent hypoglycemia** desensitizes the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal axis
2. **Reduced catecholamine response** to falling glucose
3. **Altered glycemic thresholds** for symptom recognition

## Management

- Strict avoidance of hypoglycemia for 2-4 weeks
- Use of ultra-long-acting analogues (degludec) with flatter profiles
- Continuous glucose monitoring with alarms
- Insulin pump therapy with predictive low-glucose suspend
