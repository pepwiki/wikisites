# Nociceptin

## Overview

Endogenous ORL1 receptor agonist structurally related to dynorphin.

## Structure

| Property | Value |
| -------- | ----- |

## Mechanism of Action

ORL1 agonist with distinct pain-modulating effects opposite to classical opioids in some pathways.
