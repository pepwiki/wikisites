## Overview

ACTH Family Peptides are derived from proopiomelanocortin (POMC) and regulate adrenal function and pigmentation.

## Key Members

- **ACTH**: Stimulates cortisol production
- **Alpha-MSH**: Regulates pigmentation and appetite
- **Beta-MSH**: Energy homeostasis
- **Beta-Endorphin**: Pain modulation

## Clinical Significance

ACTH deficiency causes secondary adrenal insufficiency; excess causes Cushing disease.
