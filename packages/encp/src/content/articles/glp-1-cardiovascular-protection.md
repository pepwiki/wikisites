## Key Cardiovascular Outcome Trials

### LEADER (Liraglutide)
- **HR for MACE**: 0.87 (95% CI 0.78-0.97)
- **CV death**: 0.78 (95% CI 0.66-0.93)

### SUSTAIN-6 (Semaglutide SC)
- **HR for MACE**: 0.74 (95% CI 0.58-0.95)

### SELECT (Semaglutide - Obesity)
- **HR for MACE**: 0.80 (95% CI 0.72-0.90)

### REWIND (Dulaglutide)
- **HR for MACE**: 0.88 (95% CI 0.79-0.99)

## Mechanisms of CV Protection

- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Improved endothelial function
- Plaque stabilization
- Weight loss and improved lipid profiles
