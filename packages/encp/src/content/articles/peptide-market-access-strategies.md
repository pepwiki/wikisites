## Overview

Market access for peptide therapeutics requires demonstrating clinical and economic value to payers and health technology assessment bodies.

## Key Strategies

- **Health economics**: Cost-effectiveness analysis
- **Real-world evidence**: Post-approval outcome data
- **Patient access programs**: Managed entry agreements
- **Value-based contracts**: Outcomes-based pricing

## Challenges

High acquisition costs, competition from established therapies, and payer budget impact concerns.

## GLP-1 Case Study

GLP-1 agonists demonstrate successful market access through cardiovascular outcome data and weight management benefits.
