## Overview

Peptides targeting the GI tract include both luminal and systemic agents for inflammatory, functional, and motility disorders.

## Major GI Peptides

| Drug            | Indication           | Mechanism         |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Linaclotide     | IBS-C                | GC-C agonist      |
| Plecanatide     | IBS-C, CIC           | GC-C agonist      |
| Teduglutide     | SBS                  | GLP-2 analog      |

## Clinical Applications

- Irritable bowel syndrome
- Inflammatory bowel disease
- Short bowel syndrome
- Chronic constipation
