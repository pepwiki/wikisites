options:

  - "They are degraded by gastric acid and proteolytic enzymes in the GI tract"
  - "They bind irreversibly to dietary fiber"
  - "They are actively secreted back into the intestinal lumen"
correctIndex: 1
explanation: "Oral peptide bioavailability is typically <2% due to: (1) degradation by pepsin in stomach acid, (2) digestion by pancreatic proteases (trypsin, chymotrypsin) in the duodenum, (3) brush border peptidases, and (4) poor epithelial permeability due to size and charge.
