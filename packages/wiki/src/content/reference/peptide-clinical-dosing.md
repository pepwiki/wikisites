This reference provides clinical dosing protocols for therapeutic peptides, including FDA-approved and investigational agents. Protocols include starting doses, titration schedules, maintenance doses, and special population adjustments.

## How to Use This Guide

- **Starting dose**: Initial recommended dose
- **Titration**: Dose escalation schedule
- **Maintenance**: Target therapeutic dose
- **Maximum dose**: Highest recommended dose
- **Special populations**: Renal/hepatic/pediatric/elderly adjustments

## Incretin-Based Therapies

### GLP-1 Receptor Agonists

#### Semaglutide (Ozempic, Wegovy, Rybelsus)

| Indication | Starting Dose | Titration | Maintenance | Maximum | Route |
|------------|---------------|-----------|-------------|---------|-------|
| T2DM (injection) | 0.25 mg/week | 0.25→0.5→1.0 mg | 0.5–2.0 mg/week | 2.0 mg/week | SC |
| Obesity (injection) | 0.25 mg/week | 0.25→0.5→1.0→1.7→2.4 mg | 2.4 mg/week | 2.4 mg/week | SC |
| T2DM (oral) | 3 mg/day | 3→7→14 mg | 7–14 mg/day | 14 mg/day | Oral |

**Titration schedule:**
- Week 1–4: 0.25 mg/week (initiation)
- Week 5–8: 0.5 mg/week
- Week 9–12: 1.0 mg/week (if needed)
- Week 13–16: 1.7 mg/week (if needed for obesity)
- Week 17+: 2.4 mg/week (obesity maintenance)

**Special populations:**
- **Renal impairment**: No adjustment (mild-moderate); use with caution (severe)
- **Hepatic impairment**: No adjustment (mild); use with caution (moderate-severe)
- **Pediatric**: ≥12 years (obesity); not studied <12 years
- **Elderly**: No adjustment; monitor for GI effects

### Long-Acting Insulins

| Insulin | Onset | Duration | Starting Dose | Titration | Maximum |
|---------|-------|----------|---------------|-----------|---------|
| Glargine (Lantus) | 1–2 hr | 24 hr | 10 U/day | 2 U q3days | 0.5 U/kg/day |
| Detemir (Levemir) | 1–2 hr | 18 hr | 10 U/day | 2 U q3days | 0.5 U/kg/day |
| Degludec (Tresiba) | 1–2 hr | 42 hr | 10 U/day | 2 U q3days | 0.5 U/kg/day |

### Insulin Titration Protocol

**Initiation:**
- HbA1c <8%: Start 10 U/day or 0.1–0.2 U/kg/day
- HbA1c 8–10%: Start 15–20 U/day or 0.2–0.3 U/kg/day
- HbA1c >10%: Start 20–30 U/day or 0.3–0.4 U/kg/day

**Titration:**
- Fasting glucose >180 mg/dL: Increase by 4 U
- Fasting glucose 140–180 mg/dL: Increase by 2 U
- Fasting glucose 110–140 mg/dL: Increase by 1 U
- Fasting glucose 80–110 mg/dL: No change
- Fasting glucose <80 mg/dL: Decrease by 2–4 U

## Special Population Adjustments

### Renal Impairment

| Peptide | Mild (GFR 60–89) | Moderate (GFR 30–59) | Severe (GFR <30) | Dialysis |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|-------------------|----------|
| Semaglutide | No adjustment | No adjustment | Use with caution | Limited data |
| Liraglutide | No adjustment | No adjustment | Use with caution | Limited data |
| GH | No adjustment | Reduce 25–50% | Reduce 50–75% | Reduce 75% |
| Insulin | No adjustment | Reduce 25% | Reduce 50% | Reduce 50% |
| Oxytocin | No adjustment | No adjustment | Use with caution | Limited data |

| Peptide | Adjustment | Monitoring |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Semaglutide | No adjustment | GI effects, hypoglycemia |

## Drug Interaction Dose Adjustments

### Insulin + Oral Hypoglycemics

| Combination | Adjustment | Monitoring |
|-------------|------------|------------|
| Insulin + sulfonylurea | Reduce sulfonylurea 50% | Glucose closely |
| Insulin + meglitinide | Reduce meglitinide 50% | Glucose closely |
| Insulin + DPP-4 inhibitor | No adjustment | Glucose periodically |
| Insulin + SGLT2 inhibitor | No adjustment | Glucose, ketones |

### GLP-1 RA + Other Drugs

| Combination | Adjustment | Monitoring |
|-------------|------------|------------|
| GLP-1 RA + sulfonylurea | Reduce sulfonylurea 50% | Glucose closely |
| GLP-1 RA + insulin | Reduce insulin 20–30% | Glucose closely |
| GLP-1 RA + warfarin | No adjustment | INR closely |

## Monitoring Parameters

### Baseline Assessment

- **Comprehensive metabolic panel**: Renal/hepatic function
- **CBC**: Baseline hematologic status
- **HbA1c**: Glycemic control (if applicable)
- **IGF-1**: GH axis baseline
- **Thyroid function**: TSH, free T4
- **Lipid panel**: Cardiovascular risk
- **Pregnancy test**: If applicable

### On-Treatment Monitoring

| Peptide | Frequency | Parameters |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| GLP-1 RAs | Every 3–6 months | HbA1c, weight, GI tolerance |
| GH | Every 3–6 months | IGF-1, glucose, thyroid |
| Insulin | Daily–weekly | Glucose, hypoglycemia |
| Oxytocin | During use | Contractions, fetal status |
| TB-500/BPC-157 | Every 3 months | CBC, inflammatory markers |
