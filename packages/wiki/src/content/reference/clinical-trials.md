This database compiles clinical trial information for peptide therapeutics across multiple therapeutic areas. Data includes trial phase, indication, sponsor, key efficacy results, and regulatory status.

## How to Use This Table

- **Phase**: I (safety), II (efficacy), III (comparative), IV (post-marketing)
- **Indication**: Primary disease or condition
- **Key Results**: Primary endpoint outcomes
- **Status**: Recruiting, completed, approved, discontinued
