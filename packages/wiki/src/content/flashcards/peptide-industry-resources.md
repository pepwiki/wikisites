front: "What regulatory pathway exists for peptide biosimilars?"
back: "In the US, peptide biosimilars follow the BPCIA (Biologics Price Competition and Innovation Act) pathway via a 351(k) BLA, requiring analytical similarity, animal studies, and clinical efficacy/safety data. In the EU, the EMA biosimilar pathway applies. Key challenges include demonstrating structural and functional similarity for complex peptides and navigating patent landscapes."
