## Community

Community features are under development. Here is what we are building.

## Contributing

We are building a contribution system for peer-reviewed content. When complete, you will be able to:

- Suggest corrections to articles
- Add citations and references
- Create new flashcards and quiz questions
- Translate content to other languages

## Quality Standards

All content follows these standards:

- Peer-reviewed sources with DOI citations
- IUPAC nomenclature for chemical names
- Molecular formulas and weight verification
- Fact-checking by domain experts

## Get Involved

This is an open educational project. If you have expertise in peptide biology, biochemistry, or pharmacology, we welcome your contributions. The content pipeline is designed for accuracy and reproducibility.

Content is authored in Markdown with structured frontmatter. All articles undergo schema validation and automated testing before deployment.
