## Key Patent Principles

1. **Composition-of-matter patents** (20 years from filing) protect the molecule itself
2. **Formulation patents** can extend effective exclusivity by 5–10 years
3. **Method-of-use patents** protect specific indications but not the molecule
4. **Device patents** protect delivery systems, not the drug
5. **Biosimilar pathways** (BPCIA in US, similar in EU) enable competition post-exclusivity
6. **Patent thickets** (multiple overlapping patents) can delay biosimilar entry
7. **Paragraph IV certifications** enable generic challenge of Orange Book patents
