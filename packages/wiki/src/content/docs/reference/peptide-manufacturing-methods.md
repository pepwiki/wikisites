# Peptide Manufacturing Methods

## Comparison

| Method | Scale | Purity | Cost | Speed | Best For |
|--------|-------|--------|------|-------|----------|
| SPPS (Fmoc) | mg-kg | >95% | Medium | Fast | Research peptides |
| SPPS (Boc) | mg-kg | >95% | Medium | Fast | Acid-sensitive sequences |
| Liquid-phase | kg-ton | >99% | Low | Slow | Commercial production |
| Recombinant | kg-ton | >99% | High initial | Medium | Long peptides, proteins |
| Native chemical ligation | mg-g | >90% | High | Slow | Difficult sequences |

## SPPS Protocol Summary

1. **Coupling**: Amino acid activation (HBTU/DIC) → amide bond formation
2. **Deprotection**: Fmoc removal with piperidine (20% in DMF)
3. **Cleavage**: TFA cocktail (95% TFA + scavengers)
4. **Purification**: RP-HPLC (acetonitrile/water梯度)
5. **Lyophilization**: Freeze-dry purified peptide
