## Selectivity Ratios

High selectivity ratios (>100:1) indicate minimal off-target activity at therapeutically relevant concentrations. Ratios <10:1 predict potential for cross-reactivity side effects.

| Receptor Family | Preferred Peptide | Selectivity Ratio | Off-Target Risk |
|-----------------|-------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| GLP-1R/GIPR | Semaglutide | >5000:1 | Low |
| Dual GLP-1R/GIPR | Tirzepatide | 1:2.1 | Moderate (balanced) |
| OTR/V1aR | Oxytocin | 300:1 | Low (antidiuretic at high doses) |
| V2R/V1aR | Desmopressin | >3000:1 | Very low |
| MC4R/MC1R | Setmelanotide | >50:1 | Low (skin darkening minimal) |
| GHSR/OTR | Ipamorelin | >1000:1 | Very low |
