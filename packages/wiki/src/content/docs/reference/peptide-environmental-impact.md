# Peptide Environmental Impact

## Solvent Usage

| Solvent | Usage | Environmental Impact | Alternative |
|---------|-------|---------------------|-------------|
| DMF | SPPS coupling | Hazardous, reproductive toxin | DMA, NMP |
| DCM | Extraction | Ozone depleting | 2-MeTHF |
| TFA | Cleavage | Corrosive, greenhouse gas | HCl/TFA mix |
| Acetonitrile | HPLC | Flammable, toxic | MeOH/Water |

## Green Chemistry Metrics

| Metric | Traditional SPPS | Green SPPS |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| Solvent waste (L/g peptide) | 100-500 | 10-50 |
| Energy consumption | High | Medium |
| Atom economy | 30-50% | 50-70% |
| E-factor | 50-100 | 10-30 |

## Waste Management

1. **Solvent recovery**: Distill and reuse DMF, DCM
2. **TFA neutralization**: Treat with calcium hydroxide before disposal
3. **Solid waste**: Incinerate at licensed facility
4. **Aqueous waste**: Treat before discharge
5. **Documentation**: Maintain waste logs per regulations
