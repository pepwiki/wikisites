## Notes

- **MW**: Molecular weight in Daltons for the peptide component; glycoconjugates (dulaglutide, semaglutide) include linker.
- **pI**: Isoelectric point; pI < 7 = acidic, pI > 7 = basic, pI ≈ 7 = neutral.
- **Net charge at pH 7.4**: Calculated from titration curves and pKa values of ionizable groups.
- **Hydrophobicity index**: Based on HPLC retention times or calculated logP; higher = more hydrophobic.
- **Solubility class**: Defined by USP/Ph. Eur. criteria at 25°C, pH 7.4 PBS.
- Half-life values are approximate and vary with formulation, route, and species.
