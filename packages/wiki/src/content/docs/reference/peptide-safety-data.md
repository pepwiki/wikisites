# Peptide Safety Data

## GLP-1 Receptor Agonists

| Peptide | Common AEs | Serious AEs | Contraindications |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Semaglutide | Nausea (20%), diarrhea, vomiting | Pancreatitis, thyroid C-cell tumors | Personal/family history of MTC, MEN2 |
| Liraglutide | Nausea (40%), diarrhea | Pancreatitis, gallbladder disease | Same as semaglutide |
| Exenatide | Nausea (35%), injection site reactions | Pancreatitis | Same as semaglutide |
| Dulaglutide | Nausea (15%), diarrhea | Pancreatitis | Same as semaglutide |

## Insulin Analogs

| Peptide | Common AEs | Serious AEs | Contraindications |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| All insulins | Hypoglycemia, weight gain, injection site reactions | Severe hypoglycemia, hypokalemia | Hypersensitivity to insulin |

## Growth Hormone Secretagogues

| Peptide | Common AEs | Serious AEs | Contraindications |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| CJC-1295 | Injection site reactions, flushing | Unknown long-term | Active malignancy |
| Ipamorelin | Headache, flushing | Unknown long-term | Same as CJC-1295 |
| GHRP-6 | Hunger, cortisol elevation | Unknown long-term | Same as CJC-1295 |

## Safety Monitoring

1. **Monitor glucose** with all GH/GLP-1 peptides
2. **Check thyroid function** with GLP-1 RAs
3. **Monitor liver function** with GH secretagogues
4. **Watch for injection site reactions** with all injectable peptides
5. **Report adverse events** to FDA MedWatch
