## Data Collection

Wikipept is a static website. We do not collect personal information, track users, or use cookies for analytics purposes.

## Local Storage

Wikipept uses browser localStorage to save your learning progress, including:

- Flashcard review history and spaced repetition schedules
- Quiz scores and session statistics
- Daily streak data

This data never leaves your browser. It is not transmitted to any server. Clearing your browser data will reset all progress.

## Third-Party Services

- **Cloudflare Pages**: Hosting and CDN (processes HTTP requests, no personal data collected)
- **Pagefind**: Client-side search index (runs entirely in your browser)

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on the project repository.
