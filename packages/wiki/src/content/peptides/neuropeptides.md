## Notes

- All sequences are single-letter amino acid codes
- `-NH2` indicates C-terminal amidation (common for bioactive peptides)
- `pGlu` or `pE` indicates pyroglutamic acid (cyclized N-terminal glutamine)
- `SO3H` indicates tyrosine sulfation (post-translational modification)
- Disulfide bridges create cyclic structures in somatostatin, oxytocin, vasopressin
- Molecular weights are approximate monoisotopic or average masses
- Many neuropeptides are co-released with classical neurotransmitters
- Bioavailability and degradation depend on neprilysin, DPP-4, and other peptidases
