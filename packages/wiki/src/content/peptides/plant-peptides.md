## Notes

- **Cyclic peptides** (cyclotides) are notable for their exceptional stability due to head-to-tail cyclization and cystine knot motif, making them excellent drug scaffolds.
- **Storage peptides** like glutenin and gliadin are relevant to celiac disease and food allergy research.
- **Toxic peptides** (abrin, ricin) are of dual-use concern and are regulated; research is focused on therapeutic immunotoxin applications.
- **Signaling peptides** represent a rapidly growing field in plant molecular biology.
- Sequences are representative; many plant peptide families have multiple isoforms and variants.
