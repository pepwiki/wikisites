## Anti-Wrinkle / Anti-Aging Peptides

### Matrixyl

**ID:** COSM-001 | **INCI Name:** Palmitoyl Pentapeptide-4 | **Sequence:** Pal-Lys-Thr-Thr-Lys-Ser

**Mechanism:** Stimulates collagen I, III, and IV synthesis; fibronectin and hyaluronic acid production via TGF-β pathway activation

**Application:** Anti-wrinkle creams, serums; clinically shown to reduce wrinkle depth by up to 44% over 2 months at 4 ppm

First generation matrikine signal peptide. The palmitoyl group enhances lipophilicity and skin penetration. Fragments of collagen type I C-terminal propeptide.

---

### Matrixyl 3000

**ID:** COSM-002 | **INCI Name:** Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1 / Palmitoyl Tetrapeptide-7 | **Sequence:** Pal-Gly-His-Lys (pal-GHK) + Pal-Gly-Gln-Pro-Arg

**Mechanism:** Pal-GHK stimulates collagen/GAG synthesis (matrikine); Pal-GQPR reduces IL-6-mediated inflammation

**Application:** Anti-aging serums, eye creams; reduces deep wrinkles; anti-inflammatory synergy for sensitive/aging skin

Dual peptide complex combining collagen stimulation (pal-GHK, fragment of α2(I) collagen) with anti-inflammatory action (pal-GQPR, fragment of immunoglobulin G). More comprehensive than original Matrixyl.

---

### Argireline

**ID:** COSM-003 | **INCI Name:** Acetyl Hexapeptide-3 (Ac-Glu-Glu-Met-Gln-Arg-Arg-NH₂) | **Sequence:** Ac-Glu-Glu-Met-Gln-Arg-Arg-NH₂

**Mechanism:** SNARE complex modulator; inhibits vesicle docking by competing with SNAP-25 for SNARE complex assembly

**Application:** "Botox-like" wrinkle relaxers; eye contour, forehead serums; non-injection alternative to botulinum toxin

Hexapeptide derived from the N-terminal of SNAP-25. Reduces expression line depth by ~30% at 10% concentration over 30 days. Does not paralyze muscle; modulates neurotransmitter release.

---

### SNAP-8

**ID:** COSM-004 | **INCI Name:** Acetyl Octapeptide-3 | **Sequence:** Ac-Glu-Glu-Met-Gln-Arg-Arg-Ala-Asp-NH₂

**Mechanism:** Enhanced SNARE complex modulation; longer SNAP-25 mimic with improved potency over Argireline

**Application:** Anti-wrinkle serums; expression line treatment; marketed as next-generation Argireline alternative

Extended version of Argireline with two additional amino acids (Ala-Asp). Reportedly 30% more effective than Argireline at same concentration for reducing expression line depth.

---

### Syn-Hycan

**ID:** COSM-005 | **INCI Name:** Tetradecyl Aminobutyroylvalylaminobutyric Urea Trifluoroacetate | **Sequence:** Modified tetrapeptide with urea bridge

**Mechanism:** Stimulates hyaluronan (HA) synthesis; upregulates hyaluronan synthase 2 (HAS2); promotes HA-mediated dermal restructuring

**Application:** Anti-aging treatments targeting volume loss; sagging skin; dermal density restoration

Targets the hyaluronan-rich extracellular matrix. Promotes high-molecular-weight HA production for improved skin hydration and turgor.

---

### Syn-Tacks

**ID:** COSM-006 | **INCI Name:** Palmitoyl Dipeptide-5 Diaminobutyroyl Hydroxythreonine + Palmitoyl Dipeptide-5 Diaminohydroxybutyrate | **Sequence:** Dual palmitoyl dipeptide complex

**Mechanism:** Stimulates dermal-epidermal junction (DEJ) components: laminin-5, collagen IV, integrin, and nidogen synthesis

**Application:** Anti-aging for skin laxity; strengthens dermal-epidermal cohesion; improves skin texture and firmness

Targets the basement membrane zone. Improves dermal-epidermal communication, which deteriorates with photoaging and chronological aging.

---

### GHK-Cu

**ID:** COSM-007 | **INCI Name:** Copper Tripeptide-1 | **Sequence:** Gly-His-Lys (GHK) chelated to Cu²⁺

**Mechanism:** Multi-target: stimulates collagen/elastin/GAG synthesis; promotes angiogenesis; antioxidant (SOD-like); anti-inflammatory; attracts immune and stem cells; modulates MMP/TIMP balance

**Application:** Anti-aging serums; scar treatment; post-procedure recovery; hair growth; wound healing

Naturally occurring tripeptide-copper complex found in plasma (~200 ng/mL young adults, declining with age). Most clinically documented cosmetic peptide with >50 years of research.

---

### Luminescine

**ID:** COSM-008 | **INCI Name:** Hydrolyzed Verbascum Thapsus Flower (standardized to verbascoside-peptide complex) | **Sequence:** Phenylpropanoid glycoside-peptide (verbascoside core)

**Mechanism:** Converts UV radiation into visible light (photoluminescence); free radical scavenging; protects fibroblasts from UV damage

**Application:** Sun care; anti-aging foundations; products for radiant/luminous skin tone

Derived from *Verbascum thapsus* (mullein) flowers. Acts as a photoluminescent filter, transforming UV photons into beneficial visible light while protecting skin cells.

## Muscle-Relaxant Peptides

### Leuphasyl

**ID:** COSM-009 | **INCI Name:** Pentapeptide-18 | **Sequence:** Tyr-Ala-Gly-Phe-Leu

**Mechanism:** Targets enkephalin receptor on nerve endings; modulates acetylcholine release at the neuromuscular junction (different mechanism from Argireline)

**Application:** Expression line relaxers; often combined with Argireline for synergistic "botox-like" effect

Enkephalin-mimetic pentapeptide. Works at a different stage of vesicle release than SNARE-targeting peptides. Synergistic when combined with Argireline (different mechanism of action).

---

### Syn-Ake

**ID:** COSM-010 | **INCI Name:** Dipeptide Diaminobutyroyl Benzylamide Diacetate | **Sequence:** β-Ala-Pro-Dab-NHBzl (synthetic tripeptide amide)

**Mechanism:** Competitive antagonist of muscle-type nicotinic acetylcholine receptor (nAChR); mimics Waglerin-1 (Temple Viper venom peptide)

**Application:** Anti-wrinkle serums; expression line treatment; forehead and crow's feet

Synthetic peptide mimicking Waglerin-1 from *Tropidolaemus wagleri* venom. Blocks nAChR at the neuromuscular junction, reducing muscle contraction. Winner of Swiss Technology Award 2006.

## Collagen / Extracellular Matrix Peptides

### Biopeptide EL

**ID:** COSM-011 | **INCI Name:** Palmitoyl Tripeptide-5 | **Sequence:** Pal-Lys-Val-Lys (pal-KVK)

**Mechanism:** TGF-β mimetic; stimulates collagen I synthesis; activates type I TGF-β receptor signaling without cytokine

**Application:** Anti-aging creams; firming treatments; wrinkle reduction

Signal peptide fragment that mimics TGF-β binding to its receptor. Stimulates collagen production via the natural repair pathway.

---

### Collaxyl

**ID:** COSM-012 | **INCI Name:** Hexapeptide-9 | **Sequence:** Pro-Gly-Pro-Gln-Gly-Gln

**Mechanism:** Collagen-derived matrikine; stimulates collagen I, III, IV, fibronectin, and laminin synthesis; promotes keratinocyte differentiation

**Application:** Anti-aging; skin repair; post-sun damage treatment; barrier strengthening

Sequence derived from collagen α1(I) chain fragment. Supports both collagen synthesis and epidermal differentiation.

---

### Kollaren

**ID:** COSM-013 | **INCI Name:** Tripeptide-9 Citrulline + Tripeptide-10 Citrulline | **Sequence:** Modified collagen fragment with citrulline

**Mechanism:** Protects collagen from MMP-1 and MMP-3 degradation; competitive inhibitor of collagenase binding to collagen fibrils

**Application:** Anti-aging; photoaging treatment; protects existing collagen matrix

"Decoy" peptide that occupies MMP binding sites on collagen, preventing enzymatic degradation. Protects rather than rebuilds.

---

## Pigmentation Peptides

### Melanostatine

**ID:** COSM-014 | **INCI Name:** Nonapeptide-1 | **Sequence:** Tyr-Val-Arg-Gly-Ser-Ser-Ser-Val-Pro (α-MSH antagonist fragment)

**Mechanism:** Competitive antagonist of melanocortin-1 receptor (MC1R); blocks α-MSH-induced melanogenesis without cytotoxicity

**Application:** Skin brightening; hyperpigmentation treatment; dark spot correction; safe alternative to hydroquinone

Synthetic peptide that blocks the α-MSH/MC1R signaling cascade at the receptor level. Inhibits melanin synthesis without destroying melanocytes.

## Eye Care Peptides

### Eyeseryl

**ID:** COSM-015 | **INCI Name:** Acetyl Tetrapeptide-5 | **Sequence:** Ac-β-Ala-His-Ser-His-OH

**Mechanism:** Anti-edema: inhibits glycation of hemoglobin and collagen IV; reduces vascular permeability; improves lymphatic drainage; anti-inflammatory

**Application:** Eye contour products; reduces under-eye puffiness/bags; dark circle treatment

Targets the three main causes of under-eye bags: fluid accumulation (edema), collagen glycation (skin elasticity loss), and vascular congestion. Reduces under-eye volume by up to 70% in clinical studies.
