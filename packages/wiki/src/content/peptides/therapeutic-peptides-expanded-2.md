## Key Notes

- **Peptide-derived small molecules** (sotorasib, adagrasib, tepotinib) are included for their peptide-derivation origins despite being classified as small molecules.
- **Antibody-drug conjugates** (trastuzumab deruxtecan, sacituzumab tirumotecan, datopotamab deruxtecan) use peptide or peptide-like linkers and are relevant to peptide therapeutics design.
- **Antisense oligonucleotides** (eteplirsen, golodirsen, casimersen, viltolarsen, nusinersen) use synthetic nucleic acid backbones and share design principles with therapeutic peptides.
- **Oral GLP-1 agonists** (orforglipron, danuglipron) represent a paradigm shift in metabolic drug delivery, moving beyond injectable peptides.
- **Multi-agonist peptides** (retatrutide, survodutide, mazdutide) represent the next generation of incretin-based therapeutics with superior weight loss efficacy.
