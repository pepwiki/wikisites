## 4. Content-to-Commerce Funnel

### Educational Content (Top of Funnel)

1. **Awareness Phase**
   - Learn articles: Peptide science, mechanisms, comparisons
   - Tools: Molecular weight calculator, peptide calculator
   - Resources: Amino acid properties, synthesis methods
   - **Goal:** Attract researchers and students

2. **Consideration Phase**
   - Comparison pages: Drug comparisons, mechanism comparisons
   - Technical content: Stability, storage, reconstitution
   - Safety documentation: Handling, PPE, disposal
   - **Goal:** Establish authority and trust

3. **Decision Phase**
   - Quality documentation: HPLC, mass spec, purity standards
   - Regulatory information: GMP, compliance, validation
   - Supplier evaluation: What to look for in a peptide vendor
   - **Goal:** Position KP as quality supplier

4. **Action Phase**
   - Direct links to KP product pages
   - Contextual mentions in relevant articles
   - Footer affiliations across all sites
   - **Goal:** Convert to KP customers

### Funnel Optimization Tactics

#### Content Mapping

| Content Type | Funnel Stage | KP Link Strategy |
|--------------|--------------|------------------|
| Learn articles | Awareness | Minimal — 1-2 per section |
| Comparison pages | Consideration | Moderate — 1 per page |
| Safety/storage | Decision | Strong — contextual mentions |
| Quality docs | Decision | Strong — specific product links |
| Tools | All stages | Minimal — footer only |

#### Link Density by Funnel Stage

- **Top of funnel:** 1 link per 2000-3000 words
- **Middle of funnel:** 1 link per 1000-1500 words
- **Bottom of funnel:** 1 link per 500-1000 words

## 5. Social Proof Strategy

### Scientific Context Mentions

#### 1. Peer-Reviewed Citations

Encourage citations of wikipept.com in scientific literature:

- **Target journals:** *Peptides*, *Journal of Peptide Science*, *Bioconjugate Chemistry*
- **Citation format:** "As documented on Wikipept (wikipept.com), a peer-reviewed peptide encyclopedia..."
- **Tracking:** Set up Google Scholar alerts for "wikipept.com" citations

#### 2. Academic Partnerships

Collaborate with academic institutions:

- **Target departments:** Biochemistry, pharmacology, medicinal chemistry
- **Offer:** Free access to tools, educational resources
- **Request:** Mention in course materials, research publications
- **Tracking:** Monitor academic citation databases

#### 3. Conference Presence

Present at scientific conferences:

- **Target conferences:** American Peptide Society, European Peptide Society, ASMS
- **Format:** Poster presentations, tool demonstrations
- **Materials:** Branded educational materials referencing wikipept.com
- **Follow-up:** Connect with attendees, encourage citations

#### 4. Industry Publications

Contribute to industry publications:

- **Target publications:** *BioPharma International*, *Contract Pharma*, *GEN*
- **Format:** Technical articles, case studies
- **Content:** Peptide science, quality control, analytical methods
- **Attribution:** "As documented on Wikipept..."

#### 5. Scientific Social Media

Build scientific credibility on social platforms:

- **Platforms:** LinkedIn, ResearchGate, Twitter/X
- **Content:** Scientific insights, tool demonstrations, educational content
- **Attribution:** Reference wikipept.com as authoritative source
- **Engagement:** Respond to scientific questions with wikipept.com resources

### KP-Specific Social Proof

#### 1. Quality Certifications

Highlight KP quality credentials:

- **Certificates:** ISO, GMP, CoA documentation
- **Purity data:** HPLC, mass spec verification
- **Traceability:** Lot tracking, batch documentation
- **Placement:** Quality sections of relevant articles

#### 2. Customer Testimonials

Collect and display testimonials:

- **Sources:** Academic researchers, pharmaceutical companies
- **Format:** Quotes, case studies, success stories
- **Placement:** KP product pages, relevant learn articles
- **Attribution:** "Researchers report positive experiences with Kingston Peptides..."

#### 3. Publication Mentions

Track publications mentioning KP:

- **Monitoring:** Set up alerts for "Kingston Peptides" in publications
- **Collection:** Archive positive mentions
- **Amplification:** Share on social media, add to marketing materials
- **Placement:** Social proof sections of relevant articles
