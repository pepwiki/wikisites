## Submission Strategy

### Priority Order
1. High DA (>70): BioSpace, Science.gov, PubMed, DOAJ, AlternativeTo, Product Hunt, Crunchbase, G2
2. Medium DA (50-70): PharmaCompass, CPhI Online, ChemSpider, SaaSHub, Best of the Web
3. Lower DA (<50): Contract Pharma, BioLink, JoeAnt, Spoke, Ezilon

### Time Investment
- **Total submissions:** 20 directories
- **Estimated time:** 5-6 hours total
- **Recommended schedule:** 4-5 submissions per day over 4-5 days
