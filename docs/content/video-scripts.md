## Production Notes

### Equipment Recommendations
- **Camera:** 4K webcam or mirrorless camera
- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic (e.g., Blue Yeti)
- **Lighting:** Ring light or softbox
- **Screen recording:** OBS Studio or Camtasia
- **Animation:** After Effects or Motion
- **Editing:** DaVinci Resolve or Premiere Pro

### Branding Guidelines
- Use PepWiki color palette (primary: #0066CC, secondary: #FF6B00)
- Consistent lower thirds with PepWiki logo
- Intro/outro animation (5 seconds max)
- End screen with subscribe button and related videos
