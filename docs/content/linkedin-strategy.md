# LinkedIn Content Strategy

LinkedIn content distribution strategy for WikiPeptide professional networking and B2B engagement.

## Company Page Setup

### Step 1: Create Company Page
1. Navigate to https://www.linkedin.com/company/setup/new/
2. Select "Company" type
3. Enter company details:
   - **Name**: WikiPeptide
   - **LinkedIn URL**: linkedin.com/company/wikipeptide
   - **Industry**: Biotechnology
   - **Company size**: 2-10 employees
   - **Company type**: Privately Held

### Step 2: Complete Profile
1. **Logo**: WikiPeptide logo (300×300px minimum)
2. **Banner image**: 1128×191px with molecular imagery
3. **Tagline**: "Peptide Science Made Accessible"
4. **Description**: "WikiPeptide is a comprehensive peptide research platform providing databases, tools, and educational resources for researchers, clinicians, and pharmaceutical professionals. Our mission is to accelerate peptide science through open access to knowledge."
5. **Website**: https://wikipept.com
6. **Industry**: Biotechnology
7. **Specialties**: Peptide Synthesis, Pharmacology, Drug Development, Biochemistry, Analytical Chemistry

### Step 3: Configure Settings
- **Notifications**: Enable all
- **Analytics**: Connect to Google Analytics
- **Admin roles**: Add team members
- **Content preferences**: Science, research, education

### Step 4: Initial Content
1. **Banner**: Professional banner with molecular imagery
2. **About section**: Comprehensive description with keywords
3. **Featured content**: Link to wikipept.com
4. **Team members**: Add key personnel (if applicable)

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Schedule
| Day | Content Type | Topic |
|-----|--------------|-------|
| Monday | Text post | Educational tip or insight |
| Tuesday | Article | In-depth analysis |
| Wednesday | Text post | Industry news commentary |
| Thursday | Carousel/Data | Visualization or thread |
| Friday | Text post | Community engagement or question |

### Monthly Themes
- Week 1: Fundamentals and education
- Week 2: Industry trends and news
- Week 3: Tools and resources
- Week 4: Community and networking

### Posting Times
- **Best times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-2 PM EST
- **Avoid**: Weekends, holidays, early morning/late evening
- **Consistency**: Same time each day for algorithm favor

## Best Practices

### Do's
- Post consistently (3-5 times/week)
- Engage genuinely with others
- Share valuable, educational content
- Use high-quality images and graphics
- Respond to all comments promptly
- Join relevant LinkedIn groups
- Use hashtags strategically

### Don'ts
- Don't spam or over-promote
- Don't ignore comments or messages
- Don't post without proofreading
- Don't use irrelevant hashtags
- Don't connect without personalization
- Don't share unverified information
- Don't engage in controversial debates

## Risk Management

### Potential Issues
1. **Low engagement**: Adjust content strategy
2. **Negative comments**: Respond professionally
3. **Algorithm changes**: Stay updated on best practices
4. **Time commitment**: Batch content creation

### Mitigation Strategies
1. A/B test content types
2. Have response templates ready
3. Follow LinkedIn official blog
4. Use scheduling tools for efficiency
