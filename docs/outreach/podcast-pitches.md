## Pitch Best Practices

### Email Structure
1. **Subject line**: Specific, intriguing, includes numbers
2. **Opening**: Personal connection + why this podcast
3. **Hook**: One compelling sentence that grabs attention
4. **What I can discuss**: 5–7 bullet points
5. **Why me**: Credentials and availability
6. **Episode ideas**: 2–3 concrete formats
7. **Call to action**: Easy next step

### Timing
- Pitch 6–8 weeks before desired appearance
- Follow up after 1 week if no response
- Be flexible with scheduling
- Have availability for short-notice opportunities

### Follow-Up
- Send a thank-you note after recording
- Share the episode with your audience
- Offer to come back for follow-up topics
- Maintain the relationship for future opportunities

### Preparation
- Listen to 3–5 recent episodes before pitching
- Understand the host's style and audience
- Prepare talking points but be conversational
- Have data and sources ready for fact-checking

### Promotion
- Share episode on all social platforms
- Create clips/quotes for Twitter/X
- Write a blog post summarizing key points
- Email your newsletter subscribers
