# University Outreach Templates — Wikipept

These templates are designed for reaching out to pharmacology and biochemistry departments at universities. The goal is to introduce Wikipept as a free educational and research resource.

---

## Personalization Guide

**Before sending, research:**

1. **Recipient's recent publications** (PubMed, Google Scholar)
   - Reference a specific paper to show genuine interest
   - Connect tool relevance to their research

2. **Department focus areas**
   - Pharmacology → emphasize dosing, PK/PD tools
   - Biochemistry → emphasize structural analysis, calculations
   - Medicinal Chemistry → emphasize drug comparison, SAR tools

3. **Current courses taught**
   - Reference specific courses where tools could be integrated
   - Offer custom materials for their curriculum

4. **Recent grants or news**
   - Connect tool utility to their funded research areas
   - Time outreach around grant cycles if possible
